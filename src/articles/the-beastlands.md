# The Beastlands

The Beastlands are a plane of pure, unchained nature — home to animals and animal spirits at the fullest expression of what they are, governed only by the logic of the hunt, the cycle of predator and prey, and the overwhelming physical reality of life without civilization's mediations. The plane is neither benevolent nor hostile; it simply does not recognize the categories mortals bring to it.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented relationship, though the subject has drawn some scholarly attention. Naturalist scholars in Aru'Mas have noted similarities between the overwhelming quality of natural presence reported by visitors to the [[Vast Green]] and the character described for the Beastlands by travelers who have experienced both. Whether the planes are cosmologically connected, adjacent, or simply alike in feel is not established.
