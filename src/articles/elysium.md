# Elysium

Elysium is a plane of transcendent good — rolling fields and peaceful rivers under golden light, where goodness is not passive but present as a quality of the land itself, active and warm in everything it touches. It is the resting place of righteous souls in many cosmological traditions and a plane where the best of what mortals might aspire toward exists not as ideal but as condition.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented relationship. Certain contemplative orders within the [[Pathite Pantheon]] — particularly those oriented toward healing, mercy, or afterlife theology — describe conceptual parallels between their understanding of divine reward and what records say of Elysium, but no formal connection between Ahvantir's theological traditions and this plane has been established.
