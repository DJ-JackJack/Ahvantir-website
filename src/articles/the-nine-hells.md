# The Nine Hells

The Nine Hells of Baator are the most rigidly organized expression of evil in the known cosmology: nine descending layers, each governed by an archdevil, each more oppressive and more precisely structured than the last. Devils operate on contracts, hierarchy, and the careful corruption of law into an instrument of cruelty; their ambitions are political in a way most demonic evil is not, and their patience is calculated in centuries.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented relationship, though the subject surfaces periodically in Aru'Mas's commercial underground. Rumors of infernally tainted contracts, devil-touched merchants, or Hellish artifacts passing through the city's trade networks have persisted without being confirmed. No formal infernal incursion or documented Nine Hells connection has appeared in accessible scholarship.
