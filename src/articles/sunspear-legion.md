# [[Sunspear Legion]]

The Sunspear Legion is structured as a well-organized and multifaceted force, divided into several specialized units that allow it to handle the unique challenges of Aru’Mas, both within the city walls and in its surrounding territories.

## 1. High Command

- Leadership: The High Command of the City Guard is led by the High Captain of the Sunspears, who reports directly to the ruling council of [[Aru'Mas]] . The current High Captain is traditionally appointed by the monarch, with input from the [[Merchants Guild|Merchants' Guild]] and [[The Church of the Threefold Path|the Church of the Threefold Path]], ensuring that the leader can balance the needs of the nobility, the working class, and the religious factions.
- Advisory Council: Supporting the High Captain is a council of senior officers known as the Council of Spears, which includes representatives from each major unit of the guard. This council meets regularly to strategize, assess threats, and manage both the city’s defenses and its ceremonial duties.
- Garrison Commanders: Beneath the High Captain are Garrison Commanders, who oversee individual districts of Aru’Mas. Each commander is responsible for both maintaining order and responding to threats in their specific region.

## 2. Units and Divisions

The City Guard is divided into several specialized units, each trained for a different aspect of the city’s defense and security:

- The [[Skywardens]] :
- Role: The most famous and elite unit within the Sunspear Legion, the Skywardens are the aerial cavalry of Aru'Mas , renowned for their use of flying mounts. They protect the city’s airspace, conduct long-range patrols, and engage in high-risk operations, including countering airborne threats and magical incursions.
- Training: Skywardens undergo extensive training that includes aerial combat, arcane communication with mounts, and joint tactics with ground forces. They form the front line of the city's aerial defenses and often perform in grand aerial displays during city festivals, showcasing their skill and the capabilities of their mounts.
- Mounts: The Skywardens ride a variety of unique flying creatures, such as [[Stormtalons]], [[Aetherwings]], [[Drakehawks]], and [[Skyblades]], each chosen based on the rider's role and combat style.
- The [[Ironshields]] :
- Role: The Ironshields are the core infantry of the City Guard, responsible for maintaining order within the city’s districts, managing checkpoints, and providing rapid response to internal conflicts. They are skilled in both melee combat and urban tactics, making them adept at handling street fights, criminal activities, and riots.
- Training: Ironshields are trained to use a wide range of weapons, from spears and swords to crossbows and shields. They are also trained in anti-magic tactics, as magical disturbances are common in a multiversal hub like Aru'Mas .
- Special Equipment: They carry warded shields designed to protect against both physical attacks and minor magical spells, along with enchanted spears (or Sunspears) that glow with the light of Solara, capable of dispelling dark energies.
- The [[Nightcloaks]] :
- Role: The Nightcloaks serve as the stealth and intelligence unit of the Sunspear Legion. They specialize in covert operations, espionage, and the neutralization of internal threats, such as assassins, rogue mages, or members of the [[Red Masquerade]] —a vampiric cult that threatens the city’s underworld.
- Training: Nightcloaks are masters of stealth, disguise, and silent combat. They also receive training in shadow magic and illusion, which aids them in remaining unseen during their missions.
- Gear: Nightcloaks are equipped with shadow-infused armor that blends with darkness, allowing them to move unseen. They also use enchanted daggers and smoke bombs for stealthy takedowns and distractions.
- The [[Runeguard]] :
- Role: The Runeguard is the arcane division of the City Guard, responsible for dealing with magical threats, arcane disruptions, and the protection of important relics and magical artifacts within the city.
- Training: Runeguard members are skilled in battle magic, wards, and counter-spell techniques. They work closely with the [[Order of Wisdom]] to study and counter magical threats, such as rogue spellcasters, summoned creatures, or unstable portals.
- Special Equipment: Runeguard members wield runic staves and wear warded armor enchanted to resist magical attacks. They are also equipped with spellbreakers, small talismans that can disrupt hostile magic in their vicinity.
- The [[Vanguard of Valor]] :
- Role: The Vanguard is a heavily armed unit that serves as the shock troops of the Sunspear Legion. They are deployed during large-scale battles, sieges, or when overwhelming force is needed, such as when facing external invaders or powerful foes from the Ahvantir Archipelago.
- Training: Vanguards are trained for brutal combat, with an emphasis on both offense and defense. They excel in holding the line against overwhelming odds, making them the backbone of Aru’Mas’ defense during external assaults.
- Gear: Vanguards wear heavy armor, often enchanted with defensive runes, and wield great weapons such as halberds, mauls, and two-handed swords. Their armor is marked with the emblem of Nystara, symbolizing their unyielding resolve in the face of darkness.

## Role and Duties of the Sunspear Legion

## 1. Defense of the City

- The primary duty of the City Guard is to defend Aru’Mas against internal and external threats. This includes:
- Patrolling the city’s streets, enforcing laws, and maintaining peace.
- Guarding the walls, ensuring that no unauthorized individuals enter or leave the city.
- Engaging in battles against external threats, such as invaders, rogue adventurers, or magical anomalies from the Ahvantir Archipelago.
- Protecting key landmarks, such as the Royal Palace, the Temples of the Church of the Threefold Path, and the Merchants' Guild Halls.

## 2. Ceremonial Duties

- The Sunspear Legion is also responsible for representing Aru’Mas in ceremonial roles, including:
- Aerial parades and demonstrations during major city festivals, such as the Dawn of Solara or the Day of Unity.
- Guarding the ruling council and attending official ceremonies to ensure the safety of high-ranking officials and foreign dignitaries.
- Participating in diplomatic missions, often as part of a show of force or to escort important delegations safely through the multiversal realms.

## 3. Specialized Missions

- Due to the unique nature of Aru’Mas as a multiversal hub, the Sunspear Legion often engages in missions that require multiversal travel, exploration, or defense against otherworldly threats. These missions include:
- Patrolling the archipelago of Ahvantir, dealing with rogue factions, magical disturbances, and dangerous creatures that threaten the city’s borders.
- Multiversal missions to establish or protect trade routes, defend allies, or enforce the city’s interests across different realms.
- Controlling magical anomalies within the city, such as unstable portals, rogue constructs, or summoned entities that pose a risk to public safety.

## Training and Recruitment

## Recruitment Process

- The Sunspear Legion is highly selective, with a recruitment process that tests potential members on combat skills, magical aptitude, and discipline. Recruits often undergo a trial period where they must prove themselves capable of handling both mundane and magical threats.
- New recruits are usually drawn from adventurers’ guilds, the [[Order of Valor]], and other military groups within the city. Adventurers who prove their valor in battle are often invited to join the City Guard, offering them a chance to serve the city more formally.

## Training Programs

- The Spear Academy is the training center for all new recruits, located within the Citadel of Solara in the heart of Aru’Mas. It offers rigorous programs that cover:
- Combat skills, including swordsmanship, archery, and mounted combat.
- Magical training, focusing on wards, counter-spells, and protective enchantments.
- Mount training for the Skywardens , involving aerial maneuvers, communication with flying mounts, and combat tactics in the air.

## Public Perception of the Sunspear Legion

The Sunspear Legion is widely respected by the people of Aru’Mas, seen as a symbol of order, power, and honor. The guard’s presence inspires confidence among the city’s residents, who view them as both protectors and paragons of the city’s adventurous spirit. However, their role as enforcers also means that they must maintain a delicate balance between strict discipline and understanding the free-spirited nature of the city’s populace.

The aerial displays put on by the Skywardens during festivals and ceremonies not only demonstrate the martial might of the Sunspear Legion but also serve as powerful symbols of the city’s unity, strength, and defiance against the challenges of the multiverse.
