# High Priest Alabaster Sayn

- Title: High Priest of [[The Church of the Threefold Path|the Church of the Threefold Path]]
- Age: 55
- Affiliation: The Church of the Threefold Path, the ruling council of Aru’Mas
- Base of Operations: The [[Heartspire]], the spiritual center of the city, located near the inner sanctum of Aru’Mas.

## Overview

High Priest Alabaster Sayn is the current spiritual leader of the Church of the Threefold Path, the most influential religious institution in Aru’Mas, and holds the title of **Arbiter** — the apex of the Church’s hierarchy. As Arbiter, Alabaster does not answer to any single Order but instead serves as the direct voice of the consensus of all three deities: [[Vesira]], [[Orenn]], and [[Alor]]. He sits above the three Order leaders (the High Hierophant, High Oracle, and High Warden), mediating between their domains and ensuring the unified will of the Threefold Path is expressed in all matters of doctrine, diplomacy, and governance. Known for his charismatic leadership, shrewd diplomacy, and unwavering dedication to the three guiding virtues of the church—Harmony, Wisdom, and Valor—Alabaster is both a spiritual beacon and a political figure. He represents the Church on the ruling council, playing a crucial role in maintaining the Old Pacts, guiding the city’s spiritual policies, and preserving balance between the spiritual forces of Ahvantir and the people of Aru’Mas.

## Background and Rise to Power

Born to a prominent family of scholars in Aru’Mas, Alabaster Sayn was raised with a deep respect for the city’s spiritual laws and the teachings of the Threefold Path. Early in his life, he showed a talent for spiritual attunement and arcane diplomacy, qualities that eventually led him to join the [[Order of Harmony]], the branch of the church devoted to healing, peace, and spiritual mediation.

Alabaster’s rise within the church was marked by both his visionary reforms and his willingness to confront complex spiritual issues head-on. He gained a reputation as a diplomatic mediator, skillfully negotiating peace between conflicting spiritual factions and resolving disputes within the city’s various factions. His ability to communicate with spirits—combined with his talent for ritual magic—earned him the respect of both the clergy and the ruling council.

Alabaster became High Priest of the Church of the Threefold Path after leading the successful Restoration of the Heartspire, a ritual that renewed the city’s central spiritual wards and reaffirmed the Old Pacts. His ascension to this position was seen as both a spiritual blessing and a political victory, solidifying his status as a central figure in Aru’Mas’ governance.

## Goals and Ambitions

As High Priest, Alabaster’s primary goals are deeply tied to the spiritual well-being of Aru’Mas and the preservation of the Old Pacts.

## 1. Preservation of the Old Pacts

- Alabaster is committed to maintaining the Old Pacts, the foundational agreements between the city’s people and the [[Spirits of Ahvantir|spirits of Ahvantir]]. He believes that these pacts are not just ancient traditions, but living agreements that ensure the city’s safety and prosperity.
- He is actively involved in the annual Ritual of Renewal at the Heartspire, a grand ceremony that reinforces the Old Pacts and secures the spiritual balance of the city. Alabaster also leads negotiations with spirits when new pacts are needed, either for expanded spiritual protection or to resolve conflicts that arise within the city’s boundaries.

## 2. Expansion of Spiritual Influence

- While Alabaster prioritizes maintaining balance, he also seeks to expand the church’s influence within the city. He has initiated several programs that offer free healing, education, and ritual training to residents, aiming to strengthen the city’s spiritual foundation.
- Alabaster wants to ensure that the Church of the Threefold Path plays a more active role in the city’s diplomacy, both with multiversal visitors and with the various guilds and factions that shape Aru’Mas. He believes that spiritual guidance should inform not just internal policies, but also Aru’Mas’ interactions with the broader multiverse.

## 3. Strengthening Spiritual Alliances

- Alabaster believes that the spiritual forces of Ahvantir are key allies, not obstacles, in the city’s growth. He advocates for diplomatic engagement with powerful spirits, aiming to form new pacts that could provide arcane insights or enhanced protection against multiversal threats.
- He seeks to build stronger alliances with the [[Order of Wisdom]], encouraging cooperation between scholars and spiritual mediators in understanding and harnessing Ahvantir’s spiritual energy.

## Personal Life and Family

Despite his role as High Priest, Alabaster maintains a complex personal life, balancing his spiritual duties with personal connections that have significant implications for Aru’Mas’ political landscape.

## 1. Family Ties

- Alabaster has a son, Kallian Sayn, who is a prominent member of the [[Order of Valor]]. Kallian is a talented warrior-priest, known for his skill in spiritual combat and his dedication to defending the city. The relationship between father and son is one of mutual respect, but also marked by tension—Kallian’s aggressive tactics often clash with Alabaster’s more diplomatic approach.
- Alabaster is also rumored to have a distant cousin, Miryen Sayn, who is involved with the Nightblades, an adventurers' guild known for its shadowy dealings. While Alabaster publicly distances himself from Miryen’s activities, their family connection complicates his political alliances, particularly with factions wary of the Nightblades’ methods.

## 2. Personal Beliefs and Practices

- Alabaster is a devout practitioner of the Threefold Path, adhering strictly to its principles of harmony, wisdom, and valor. He regularly participates in personal rituals to maintain his spiritual attunement, often spending hours in meditation at the Heartspire.
- Despite his position of power, Alabaster remains approachable, often seen engaging directly with citizens during festivals or rituals. He believes in the importance of being a visible leader, fostering trust between the church and the people of Aru’Mas.

## Strengths and Weaknesses

Alabaster’s influence is defined by a complex blend of strengths and weaknesses that shape both his leadership style and his interactions within the city.

## Strengths

- Charismatic Leadership: Alabaster is a charismatic speaker and an inspiring figure. He has a talent for addressing crowds, rallying support, and negotiating even the most challenging agreements. His ability to connect with both citizens and spirits makes him a respected spiritual and political leader.
- Diplomatic Skills: Alabaster excels in diplomatic negotiations, whether with spirits, foreign dignitaries, or political rivals within the city. His focus on maintaining balance allows him to mediate effectively, often finding solutions that satisfy all parties involved.
- Deep Spiritual Knowledge: As a skilled practitioner of ritual magic and spiritual diplomacy, Alabaster has a profound understanding of the arcane forces that govern Aru’Mas. His ability to interpret spiritual omens and understand the complexities of the Old Pacts is unmatched, making him a valuable advisor to the ruling council.

## Weaknesses

- Reluctance to Embrace Aggression: Alabaster’s emphasis on diplomacy can sometimes be seen as a weakness by more aggressive factions, particularly the Sunsworn Fellowship and [[Heir Apparent|Crown Prince Kaelen]]. His reluctance to resort to force, even when faced with threats, has led to criticism that he is too passive in defending the city’s interests.
- Vulnerability to Manipulation: Alabaster’s dedication to maintaining harmony can be exploited by those willing to manipulate spiritual diplomacy for their own gain. He has been known to overextend trust, believing that peace is always achievable, which has occasionally left him vulnerable to betrayal.
- Family Tensions: Alabaster’s relationship with his son, Kallian, and his cousin, Miryen, complicates his political alliances. While he tries to keep family matters separate from church affairs, his personal connections can lead to conflicts of interest, particularly when it comes to issues of spiritual warfare or covert operations.

## Political Alliances and Conflicts

As a key member of the ruling council, Alabaster interacts closely with other political figures, both aligning with and opposing them based on the circumstances.

## Alliances

- Queen [[Thara Ilderas]]: Alabaster shares a strong alliance with Queen Thara, who supports his spiritual diplomacy and the church’s outreach programs. The two often collaborate on matters of healing, education, and maintaining the city’s spiritual balance.
- [[Scholar Princess|Princess Lira]] Ilderas: Alabaster maintains a close working relationship with Princess Lira, valuing her scholarly insights and commitment to the spiritual aspects of Aru’Mas. The two often collaborate on research and spiritual diplomacy, making them a strong force for arcane understanding and spiritual protection.

## Conflicts

- Crown Prince Kaelen Ilderas: Alabaster’s diplomatic approach often puts him at odds with Kaelen, whose aggressive strategies prioritize military dominance over spiritual harmony. Their relationship is marked by frequent disagreements, particularly when it comes to expansionist tactics that could endanger the Old Pacts.
