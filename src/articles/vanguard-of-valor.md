# Vanguard of Valor

The Vanguard of Valor are the elite warriors of the [[Sunspear Legion]] , known for their unmatched skill in close-quarters combat and their unwavering dedication to the defense of [[Aru'Mas]] and its ruling royal family, the [[Ilderas Dynasty]] . As the personal guards of the royal family, the Vanguard of Valor represent the highest ideals of honor, loyalty, and courage. They serve as both the first line of defense in times of crisis and the last line of protection for the monarchy, making them one of the most prestigious and respected units in the city’s military forces.

## Role and Purpose

The Vanguard of Valor fulfill multiple roles in defense, protection, and direct combat, ensuring the safety and stability of both the city and its royal family:

## 1. Royal Guardians

- The Vanguard of Valor are the personal protectors of the Ilderas family, tasked with defending the king, queen, and their children from any threats—both mundane and magical. Members of the Vanguard are stationed in the royal palace, at official events, and during public appearances to ensure the safety of the royal family.
- They form an unbreakable shield around the monarch and his heirs, using their formidable combat skills to repel assassins, magical threats, and other dangers that may arise in the ever-turbulent political landscape of Aru’Mas.
- In addition to physical protection, they also serve as advisors and confidants to the royal family, trusted to accompany the monarch during critical negotiations or high-level meetings where security is paramount.

## 2. Elite Strike Force

- The Vanguard of Valor are the shock troops of the [[Sunspear Legion]] , deployed in situations where decisive and overwhelming force is required. They excel in close-quarters combat, breaching fortified positions, and leading charges in both urban and open-field engagements.
- They often lead the charge in major battles, breaking through enemy lines and securing critical objectives. Their presence on the battlefield serves as a rallying symbol for allied troops, boosting morale and reinforcing the city’s military might.
- During sieges or defensive actions, the Vanguard are the first to respond to breaches, using their superior training and enchanted weapons to hold key positions and repel enemy incursions.

## 3. Special Missions and Diplomatic Escorts

- The Vanguard of Valor are frequently assigned to special missions, including high-risk rescues, securing multiversal envoys, and neutralizing powerful threats that require their exceptional skills. Their experience in both combat and protection makes them ideal for delicate operations where both force and diplomacy may be needed.
- As the guardians of the monarch, they often act as diplomatic escorts during important missions beyond the city’s borders, particularly in regions where the Old Pacts are in flux. Their presence signals the authority of the royal family and reinforces the importance of the mission at hand.

## Vanguard Gear and Equipment

The Vanguard of Valor are distinguished by their exquisite armor, weapons, and other gear, each infused with powerful enchantments designed to maximize their effectiveness in both protection and combat.

## 1. Valorplate Armor

- Members of the Vanguard wear Valorplate Armor, a suit of enchanted plate armor made from blessed runesteel, an alloy known for its durability and resistance to both physical and magical attacks. Each suit is custom-fitted to its wearer and adorned with symbols of royal authority, often marked with the Ilderas crest.
- The armor is enchanted with runes that enhance the wearer’s strength, speed, and endurance, making them faster and more resilient in battle. It also provides protection against magical attacks, with built-in wards that absorb arcane energy and deflect curses or hexes.
- In addition to its combat functionality, the Valorplate is designed to be a symbol of authority, projecting an aura of confidence and power that reinforces the Vanguard’s role as royal protectors.

## 2. Sunblades

- The primary weapon of the Vanguard is the Sunblade, a masterwork longsword or greatsword imbued with solar energy. Sunblades are forged using radiant runes that allow them to emit bursts of sunlight, making them particularly effective against dark magic, spiritual entities, and other creatures vulnerable to radiant energy.
- The blades have the ability to unleash powerful solar strikes, releasing concentrated beams of radiant light that can cut through armor, magical barriers, and even spirit-infused wards. In the hands of a skilled Vanguard warrior, a Sunblade is a weapon of immense offensive power and symbolic significance.

## 3. Aegis Shields

- Vanguard warriors carry Aegis Shields, large, kite-shaped shields that are enchanted for both defense and utility. These shields are capable of expanding into full-body barriers, providing complete protection against incoming attacks, including spells, arrows, and physical strikes.
- The shields are inscribed with protection runes, allowing them to absorb magical energy and redirect it as counterattacks. This defensive versatility makes the Aegis Shields vital for protecting both the Vanguard and the royal family during sudden ambushes or intense combat.

## Training and Organization

The Vanguard of Valor undergo some of the most rigorous and specialized training in the Sunspear Legion , combining martial discipline, magical aptitude, and unwavering loyalty to the royal family.

## 1. Recruitment and Selection

- Only the most skilled and loyal warriors are considered for recruitment into the Vanguard of Valor. Potential candidates are drawn from the ranks of the Sunspear Legion , the [[Runeguard]] , and other military divisions known for producing elite fighters. Candidates are carefully vetted for both combat ability and personal loyalty to the royal family.
- Recruits must pass a series of tests known as the Trials of Radiance, which include intense combat drills, defensive formations under duress, and simulations of assassination attempts. These trials are designed to ensure that candidates can perform under extreme pressure and protect the monarch in even the most chaotic situations.

## 2. Training Regimen

- Vanguard training emphasizes close-quarters combat, with a focus on using both Sunblades and Aegis Shields to create a seamless blend of offense and defense. Recruits learn to execute rapid strikes, block incoming attacks, and create openings for their allies.
- In addition to physical training, recruits undergo rigorous mental conditioning to reinforce their loyalty to the royal family. This includes studying the history of the Ilderas Dynasty , understanding the intricacies of Aru’Mas’ political landscape, and learning to anticipate potential threats to the monarchy.
- Training also includes tactics for bodyguard duty, such as positioning during public events, evacuation protocols, and methods for detecting and neutralizing threats in crowds or confined spaces.

## 3. Structure and Command

- The Vanguard of Valor are led by the Champion of the Crown, currently Champion Seran Marik, a legendary warrior known for his tactical brilliance and personal dedication to the royal family. Seran has served the Ilderas line for over two decades, earning his position after saving [[Voren Ilderas|King Voren]]’s life during an assassination attempt.
- The Vanguard are organized into Companies, each led by a Company Captain who commands a group of 10-15 warriors. Each company is assigned specific responsibilities, such as palace defense, escort duty, or field operations.
- Within each Company, the Vanguard are further divided into Squads, smaller teams of 4-5 warriors trained to operate as cohesive units. Squads are designed for versatility, with members possessing a mix of skills in swordsmanship, defensive magic, and protective rituals, ensuring that they can adapt to a variety of threats.

## Missions and Duties

The Vanguard of Valor are involved in a range of missions that emphasize their roles as royal protectors, elite warriors, and symbols of the monarchy’s power:

## 1. Palace Security and Royal Protection

- The Vanguard maintain a constant presence within the royal palace, providing round-the-clock security for the Ilderas family. They are stationed at key points within the palace, including the royal chambers, council halls, and treasury, ready to respond to any threat at a moment’s notice.
- During public appearances or official events, the Vanguard form a protective perimeter around the royal family, using their shields and combat skills to fend off potential attacks. Their presence is a visible symbol of strength and authority, ensuring that no one doubts the power of the monarchy.

## 2. High-Risk Combat Missions

- In addition to royal protection, the Vanguard are often deployed for high-risk combat missions that require their exceptional skills. These missions include everything from leading assaults against enemy forces to infiltrating fortified positions and rescuing captured officials or citizens.
- The Vanguard’s blend of combat expertise and magical weaponry makes them ideal for situations where overwhelming force and rapid decision-making are needed.

## 3. Escort and Diplomacy

- The Vanguard are frequently assigned to escort the royal family or high-ranking officials during diplomatic missions, especially when negotiating with powerful spirits, rival factions, or multiversal representatives. Their presence not only ensures safety but also acts as a symbol of the monarchy’s power.
- Their role as diplomatic escorts extends beyond mere protection; they are often involved in intelligence gathering, assessing the trustworthiness of potential allies, and ensuring that negotiations remain favorable to the royal family.

## 4. Special Operations and Covert Missions

- Though primarily known for their roles as frontline warriors and royal guards, the Vanguard also conduct covert missions when necessary. These missions often involve securing dangerous artifacts, retrieving valuable intelligence, or dealing with political threats that require direct intervention.
- In such operations, the Vanguard of Valor are paired with [[Nightcloaks]] or Runeguard , combining their skills to achieve objectives that are critical to the stability and security of Aru’Mas.

## Reputation and Public Perception

The Vanguard of Valor are viewed with awe, respect, and even reverence by the people of Aru’Mas, seen as the living embodiment of the city’s courage and the loyal defenders of the throne.

## 1. Admiration and Reverence

- Citizens admire the Vanguard for their dedication, skill, and unwavering loyalty to the Ilderas family. Their ceremonial presence at royal events, combined with their reputation for bravery in battle, makes them a symbol of both power and stability in the city.
- The Vanguard’s close relationship with the royal family inspires confidence among the populace, reinforcing the idea that the monarchy is well-protected and capable of maintaining order.

## 2. Fear and Intimidation

- While the Vanguard’s dedication to protecting the monarchy is respected, their elite status and no-nonsense approach to security can be intimidating. Their loyalty to the Ilderas line is absolute, making them formidable adversaries to anyone who challenges the authority of the throne.
- For those who find themselves on the wrong side of the royal family’s favor, the Vanguard of Valor represent a terrifying force—one that strikes swiftly and without mercy.
