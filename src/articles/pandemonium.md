# Pandemonium

Pandemonium is a plane of howling, lightless tunnels carved by winds with no natural source — a place where sound amplifies until thought becomes difficult, darkness is never empty, and the suffering the plane produces is entirely impersonal. It does not punish anyone specifically. It simply does not register that anyone is there.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented relationship. Scholars studying the long-term effects of the [[Faded Veil]] on travelers — particularly its capacity for emotional drain and sensory distortion — have occasionally drawn comparisons with Pandemonium's sensory assault, but the two planes are understood as categorically distinct mechanisms.
