# Order of Harmony

The Order of Harmony is one of the three primary branches within [[The Church of the Threefold Path|the Church of the Threefold Path]] in Aru’Mas, dedicated to the deity [[Vesira]], the healer and the mother of mercy, who embodies compassion, healing, and the sanctity of life. The Order’s mission revolves around providing free healing, support, and guidance to the people of Aru’Mas, focusing on health, both physical and spiritual, and maintaining the city’s welfare. The Order plays a crucial role in the city’s social structure, offering their services freely to all residents and providing sanctuary, support, and mercy to those in need.

## Role and Responsibilities

The Order of Harmony functions as healers, spiritual guides, and social caretakers within the city, operating with the blessing and power of Vesira. They are known for their unwavering dedication to the people of [[Aru'Mas]], particularly in times of crisis.

- Healing Services: The Order offers free healing services to any resident of Aru’Mas, from minor injuries to complex ailments. Members of the Order use a combination of medicinal herbs, spiritual rituals, and Vesira’s blessings to treat their patients. Healing is performed not only to restore physical health but also to promote spiritual well-being, aligning the soul with Vesira’s mercy.
- Sanctuary and Support: The Order provides a sanctuary for the sick, poor, and weary. Their facilities are open to those in need, offering food, shelter, and a compassionate presence. They also extend help to those affected by personal misfortune, whether through illness, loss, or displacement.
- Spiritual Guidance and Counseling: Order members offer spiritual counseling to those struggling emotionally or spiritually, helping individuals find peace and balance in times of hardship. They also conduct ceremonies and rituals to invoke Vesira’s blessings for health, peace, and prosperity, helping the community maintain a spiritual connection to the divine.

## Structure and Hierarchy

The Order of Harmony is organized with a clear hierarchical structure that ensures efficient support and guidance for the people of Aru’Mas, from the highest-ranking leaders to local caretakers. Despite this hierarchy, each member is seen as integral to the Order’s mission.

## 1. High Hierophant

- The High Hierophant is the leader of the Order of Harmony, responsible for overseeing its operations and maintaining its alignment with Vesira’s teachings. This figure also represents the Order in the Council of Aru’Mas, where they advocate for the health and welfare of the people.
- Responsibilities: The High Hierophant presides over major religious ceremonies, coordinates efforts with the city’s other organizations, and makes key decisions regarding the Order’s policies and outreach. They also engage in discussions with other branches of the Church of the Threefold Path to ensure a unified mission.

## 2. Harmony Wardens

- Harmony Wardens are senior members who act as administrators and overseers within the Order. They are responsible for managing the various temples and healing centers throughout the city, ensuring each facility has the necessary resources to serve the public.
- Responsibilities: Wardens coordinate healing efforts, distribute resources to those in need, and train new acolytes in Vesira’s practices. They also oversee special rituals to consecrate Vesira’s blessings in areas affected by illness, misfortune, or strife.

## 3. Healing Acolytes

- Healing Acolytes are the primary caretakers and healers who serve directly in the temples and healing centers. They are skilled in Vesira’s blessings and trained in herbal remedies, first aid, and the spiritual practices of the Order. Acolytes often interact directly with the city’s residents, providing compassionate care and spiritual guidance.
- Responsibilities: Acolytes handle the daily healing tasks, tend to the sick and injured, offer blessings for health and protection, and perform minor rituals to invoke Vesira’s mercy. They are also responsible for preparing remedies and supplies used in the Order’s healing practices.

## 4. Lay Caretakers

- Lay Caretakers are the entry-level members and volunteers who assist with general upkeep and care within the Order’s facilities. Many caretakers aspire to become Acolytes, while others are simply devoted to helping the Order fulfill its mission.
- Responsibilities: Lay Caretakers perform practical tasks, such as preparing meals, cleaning, providing comfort to patients, and assisting with minor duties during ceremonies. They are a vital support for the Order, ensuring its facilities run smoothly and welcoming all who seek aid.

## The Deity Vesira: The Mother of Mercy

Vesira, also known as the Mother of Mercy, is the deity of healing, compassion, and forgiveness. She is revered for her boundless empathy and dedication to preserving life, and her teachings emphasize the sanctity of life and the importance of alleviating suffering wherever possible.

- Symbolism and Iconography: Vesira is often depicted as a radiant figure with a gentle, serene face, her hands extended as though offering comfort and solace. Her symbols include a silver chalice filled with water, representing renewal, and white lilies, symbolizing purity and healing.
- Doctrine and Teachings: Vesira’s teachings urge her followers to practice compassion, charity, and selflessness. She advocates for helping others without judgment and emphasizes the importance of forgiveness and understanding, even toward those who have caused harm. Healing, in Vesira’s doctrine, is an act of divine mercy, a gift to be shared with all.

## Key Rituals and Practices

The Order of Harmony’s rituals are conducted to honor Vesira and ensure her blessings flow freely to those in need. Rituals are often conducted in quiet, serene environments, creating a peaceful space for the sick and suffering to find solace and healing.

- The Ritual of Healing Waters: This is the Order’s primary healing ritual, in which blessed water infused with Vesira’s divine energy is used to cleanse and heal. The ritual involves chanting prayers and dipping a silver chalice into a consecrated pool, drawing on Vesira’s blessing to purify the water. Healing Acolytes use the water to anoint the sick, invoking Vesira’s mercy.
- The Lantern Vigil: During times of plague or widespread illness, the Order conducts a Lantern Vigil in which lamps are lit in every healing center and temple in the city. The vigil lasts until the crisis passes, symbolizing Vesira’s protective light. Each lantern is carefully tended, symbolizing the care the Order provides to those in need.
- The Blessing of the Hearth: This annual ritual is performed to secure Vesira’s protection over homes and families. Order members visit households to sprinkle blessed water around the hearth, invoking health and harmony for the coming year. Families leave offerings at their doors—flowers, food, or small tokens of thanks—to express gratitude for Vesira’s blessings.

## Interactions with the Government and Other Organizations

The Order of Harmony works closely with other organizations within Aru’Mas, including the Council of Aru’Mas and various community groups. While maintaining a stance of neutrality, the Order advocates for policies and decisions that benefit public health and social welfare.

- Council Representation: The High Hierophant sits on the Council of Aru’Mas, ensuring that the city’s decisions prioritize the well-being of its people. In Council debates, the High Hierophant often speaks for policies that support the poor, the sick, and those in need, aligning the Order’s values with Vesira’s teachings.
- Collaboration with the Merchants’ Guild: The Order often works with the Merchants’ Guild to secure resources, such as rare herbs, medicines, and supplies for their healing practices. The current Grand Speaker, [[Grand Speaker Artrice Vanderbane|Artrice Vanderbane]], has developed a professional but amicable relationship with the High Hierophant, ensuring both organizations can cooperate in times of need.
- Relationship with Other Religious Orders: The Order of Harmony collaborates closely with the other branches of the Church of the Threefold Path, working with the [[Order of Wisdom]] to educate the people about health practices and with the [[Order of Valor]] to provide medical support to the city guard and [[Sunspear Legion]].

## The Order’s Role in the Daily Lives of Aru’Mas’ People

The Order of Harmony is woven into the fabric of everyday life in Aru’Mas. People from all walks of life come to the Order for assistance, from injured workers and travelers to those seeking comfort in difficult times. The Order’s reputation for compassion, discretion, and selflessness makes them a trusted resource for all residents of the city.

- Community Gatherings: The Order frequently organizes gatherings to educate the community on health practices, spiritual well-being, and Vesira’s teachings. These gatherings are open to everyone, from city nobility to the working class, reflecting the Order’s inclusive philosophy.
- Free Healing for All: By providing free healing to all residents, the Order ensures that even the most impoverished have access to care. Their generosity is seen as a testament to Vesira’s mercy, reminding the people of Aru’Mas that health and compassion are gifts meant to be shared.

## Summary of the Order of Harmony

The Order of Harmony is a cornerstone of Aru’Mas’ spiritual and social landscape, acting as a pillar of health, compassion, and mercy under the guidance of Vesira, the Mother of Mercy. Through their hierarchical structure and dedicated members, the Order offers free healing, spiritual guidance, and refuge to all residents of the city. Their sacred rituals, charitable practices, and close ties to the community make them an integral part of Aru’Mas, embodying Vesira’s doctrine of unconditional kindness and healing.
