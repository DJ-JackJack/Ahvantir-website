# Satchel Mimics

Satchel mimics are a rare and peculiar breed of domesticated mimics found in the exotic pet shops of Aru’Mas. Unlike their wild and predatory kin, these creatures have been selectively bred and trained for companionship and utility, making them sought-after acquisitions for adventurers, merchants, and collectors alike. Their unique blend of intelligence, storage capabilities, and limited autonomy has made them a favored alternative to traditional magical storage devices such as bags of holding.

## Origins and Domestication

While the origins of satchel mimics remain mysterious, scholars at [[Arrandak Academy]] theorize that they were first tamed by skilled artificers and beastmasters who recognized their potential as living, portable storage solutions. Unlike common mimics, which rely on their ability to disguise themselves as inanimate objects to ambush prey, satchel mimics have been conditioned to form a bond with their owners, displaying loyalty and obedience. Breeding programs in Aru’Mas have refined their behaviors, ensuring they are both safe and practical companions.

## Physical Characteristics

Satchel mimics resemble ordinary satchels, backpacks, or pouches when at rest, often mimicking the textures of leather, canvas, or even ornate embroidery. Their interiors function similarly to a bag of holding, capable of storing significantly more than their outer size would suggest. When animated, a satchel mimic reveals vestigial limbs or pseudopods that it uses for limited movement and interaction. Their mouths, often hidden beneath the flap or zipper-like seam, are used more for expressing emotions than for consuming prey.

## Behavior and Intelligence

Unlike their predatory relatives, satchel mimics display remarkable adaptability and intelligence. They understand simple commands and can recognize their owner's voice, often responding with subtle shifts in texture, movement, or chirping-like sounds. Some particularly well-trained mimics can even rearrange their contents for easier retrieval, a feature highly prized by adventurers needing quick access to stored gear.

These creatures display playful and curious behavior, occasionally shifting their appearance based on their mood or environment. While generally docile, they have a mischievous streak, sometimes ‘nibbling’ on unsuspecting pickpockets or rearranging their owner's belongings when bored.

## Utility and Magical Properties

Satchel mimics are valued primarily for their combined function as storage containers and sentient assistants. Their storage capacity rivals that of a bag of holding, and their ability to follow simple commands makes them an invaluable asset for travelers. Many adventurers utilize them as mobile vaults, ensuring their possessions are not easily stolen or misplaced. Some merchants use them as secure cash carriers, while scholars and alchemists rely on them to hold fragile or dangerous components safely.

In addition to their storage capabilities, some satchel mimics have demonstrated an innate resistance to extra-dimensional hazards, making them more secure than traditional magical bags. Their ability to resist being forcefully opened or rifled through adds an additional layer of security, as they can physically close and latch themselves at their owner's command.

## Acquisition and Training

Satchel mimics are available for purchase at select exotic pet shops in Aru’Mas, most notably [[Miss Belliosa Blossom’s Emporium of Exotic Entities]]. However, acquiring one is not as simple as making a payment. Due to their intelligence, satchel mimics require a bond with their prospective owner before they can be safely entrusted to a new home. Many pet shops implement a trial period where customers interact with the mimic to ensure compatibility.

Training a satchel mimic involves a mix of positive reinforcement and structured guidance. While they instinctively understand storage and retrieval commands, advanced training can allow them to recognize individual items by name, organize their contents, or even defend their owner in minor ways, such as latching onto a thief’s arm.

## Ethical Considerations and Regulations

Despite their domestication, some scholars argue that satchel mimics should not be treated merely as magical tools but as intelligent creatures deserving of ethical treatment. The [[Order of Wisdom]] within [[The Church of the Threefold Path|the Church of the Threefold Path]] has debated whether these creatures should be classified as sentient beings or as living constructs akin to golems. Regulations in Aru’Mas require responsible ownership, with fines imposed on those who neglect or abuse their mimics.

Due to their selective breeding, wild mimics cannot be easily tamed into satchel mimics, and the sale of feral mimics disguised as domesticated ones is a crime punishable under city law.

## Conclusion

Satchel mimics stand as a testament to Aru’Mas’ ingenuity in blending magic, nature, and practical utility. Their presence in the city’s bustling markets and adventurer’s guilds highlights the evolving relationship between civilization and the arcane. For those who can form a bond with one, a satchel mimic is more than just a storage device—it is a lifelong companion, a guardian of secrets, and a marvel of magical craftsmanship. ..
