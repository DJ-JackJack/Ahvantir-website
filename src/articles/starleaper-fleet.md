# Starleaper Fleet

The Starleaper Fleet is one of the most prestigious and enigmatic facets of Aru’Mas, embodying the city’s unique position as a multiversal hub. These six magnificent vessels are equipped to traverse the Astral Sea and the vast expanse of the stars, connecting Aru’Mas to countless worlds and realms. The Fleet’s operations are steeped in both pragmatism and ambition, fulfilling roles that blend exploration, diplomacy, espionage, and commerce.

## Primary Missions of the Fleet

## 1. Recruitment of Heroes

The most public-facing role of the Starleaper Fleet is to seek out potential adventurers, scholars, and heroes from across the multiverse and invite them to Aru’Mas. The captains and crew of these vessels are charismatic representatives of the city, spreading tales of Aru’Mas’s opportunities for glory, fortune, and adventure. Their visits often inspire wonder and curiosity, drawing the bold and ambitious to follow them back to the southeastern island of Ahvantir.

In doing so, the Fleet not only reinforces the city’s reputation as a beacon for adventurers but also ensures a steady influx of talent to tackle the challenges of Ahvantir and beyond.

## 2. Transport Across the Realms

In addition to recruiting new arrivals, the Starleaper Fleet serves as a transport system for adventurers and dignitaries. Their routes often include stops in allied realms or newly discovered worlds, offering safe passage to and from Aru’Mas. Each vessel is outfitted with accommodations to ensure passengers are well-cared for during their journey, often reflecting the city’s emphasis on hospitality and inclusivity.

This transport role extends to the city’s broader diplomatic efforts, with the Fleet frequently carrying envoys, negotiators, and emissaries who represent Aru’Mas in multiversal politics.

## 3. Exploration and Intelligence Gathering

Behind the Fleet’s glamorous exterior lies a more covert purpose: the collection of data on the worlds they visit. Crews meticulously record information about:

- Local geography, culture, and magical phenomena
- Political structures and alliances
- Potential threats or enemies to Aru’Mas and Ahvantir

In worlds deemed of particular interest or concern, the Fleet discreetly leaves behind informants or agents who integrate into local societies to gather intelligence over time. These agents report back to Aru’Mas through secure and magical means, ensuring the city remains informed about potential dangers or opportunities in the multiverse.

## 4. Multiversal Trade and Commerce

The Starleaper Fleet plays a critical role in Aru’Mas’s economy, acting as couriers for goods traded between the city and its far-flung partners across the realms. These goods often include:

- Exotic materials and magical resources
- Fine crafts and art unique to Aru’Mas
- Essential supplies that bolster the city’s prosperity

By facilitating these exchanges, the Fleet strengthens Aru’Mas’s economic ties and ensures the flow of rare commodities that contribute to the city’s unique identity.

## Fleet Composition and Operation

The six ships of the Starleaper Fleet are marvels of engineering and magic, designed to withstand the rigors of the Astral Sea and the chaotic energies of interdimensional travel. Each vessel is outfitted with state-of-the-art navigational tools, magical propulsion systems, and defensive enchantments that make them both versatile and resilient.

## Crews and Captains

The crews of the Starleaper Fleet are highly skilled, comprising a mix of sailors, mages, diplomats, and warriors. Each ship is led by a captain chosen not only for their expertise but also for their ability to inspire and lead in the unpredictable circumstances of multiversal exploration.

Key roles aboard each vessel include:

- Navigators: Specialists trained in reading the shifting currents of the Astral Sea and tracking the pathways between worlds.
- Spellwrights: Mages responsible for maintaining the ship’s magical systems, including propulsion and defenses.
- Diplomatic Envoys: Crewmembers trained in cultural diplomacy, tasked with making first contact and fostering positive relations with otherworldly societies.
- Defenders: Combatants skilled in both physical and magical warfare, ready to protect the ship and its passengers from external threats.

## Ship Design

Each ship is a seamless blend of form and function, with features including:

- Astral Propulsion: A system of arcane crystals that harness astral energy to propel the ship through the Astral Sea and between realms.
- Dimensional Shields: Enchantments that protect the vessel from dangerous planar phenomena, including time distortions and void storms.
- Adaptable Interiors: The ships’ interiors shift to accommodate the needs of their passengers, with modular rooms that can transform into sleeping quarters, meeting spaces, or cargo holds as required.

## The Starleaper Fleet’s Influence on Aru’Mas

## Multiversal Reputation

The Fleet is a key factor in Aru’Mas’s reputation as a city of adventure, opportunity, and diplomacy. Its captains and crew are seen as emissaries of the city’s ideals, and their successes are celebrated as collective victories for Aru’Mas.

## Espionage and Security

While its intelligence-gathering efforts are largely secret, the Fleet’s covert operations play a crucial role in safeguarding Aru’Mas from external threats. By staying informed about potential enemies, the city can act preemptively to protect its people and maintain its standing as a multiversal hub.

## Cultural Exchange

The Fleet’s voyages also bring cultural exchange, introducing the people of Aru’Mas to new ideas, art, and traditions. The city’s Temple District and Far-Farewell Market often feature goods, performers, and practices brought back by the Fleet, enriching the city’s vibrant multicultural identity.

## Rumors and Mysteries

The Starleaper Fleet’s high-profile missions and covert operations have naturally given rise to numerous rumors and myths:

- Secret Warships: Some believe that one or more of the Fleet’s ships are secretly outfitted for war, capable of launching devastating magical attacks should the city ever come under threat.
- The Astral Lighthouse: There are tales of an Astral beacon, maintained by the Fleet, that allows ships to navigate back to Aru’Mas no matter how far they’ve traveled. Its existence is unconfirmed, but many sailors swear by its guidance.
- Lost Crews: It is whispered that some of the Fleet’s earliest voyages ended in disaster, with entire crews lost to hostile worlds or the dangers of the Astral Sea. These stories are rarely discussed openly, but they serve as a grim reminder of the risks inherent in such daring expeditions.
