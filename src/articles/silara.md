# Silara

Silara, the Veil of Secrets, is a subtle and enigmatic deity among the [[Eight Shadows]], embodying the forces of concealment, secrecy, and isolation. She thrives in the shadows, spreading mistrust and division by encouraging people to hoard knowledge, hide their true intentions, and isolate themselves from others. Silara’s power lies in the hidden and the unknown, as she believes that secrets are the ultimate form of power. She opposes the openness and transparency promoted by the [[Order of Wisdom]], viewing their ideals of shared knowledge and enlightenment as threats to her very nature.

Silara’s followers see knowledge as a resource to be controlled, hidden, and used for personal gain rather than for the benefit of the community. They are often involved in espionage, covert operations, and spreading misinformation, creating a climate of suspicion and distrust in Aru’Mas.

## Symbolism and Appearance

Silara is typically depicted as a cloaked figure with a hooded veil obscuring her face, symbolizing both the allure and danger of the unknown. Her symbol, a black crescent moon partially covered by a veil, represents her control over secrets and hidden knowledge. Her followers often carry talismans with this symbol or wear dark veils and cloaks when attending gatherings, embracing anonymity to signify their devotion to the mysteries Silara represents.

## Cults and Followers

Silara’s followers, collectively known as The Shrouded, operate in the shadows, blending into various walks of life in Aru’Mas. Unlike other cults that might revel in open displays of devotion or chaos, the Shrouded maintain their secrecy to remain hidden from the public eye. They view knowledge as a weapon, using it to manipulate events from behind the scenes, spread misinformation, and destabilize trust in the Pathite Pantheon.

## The Shrouded

The Shrouded are a network of spies, informants, and manipulators who share a devotion to Silara’s principles of secrecy and hidden influence. They value anonymity, often working in small cells where members may not even know the identities of their associates. The Shrouded are found among scholars, scribes, merchants, and even a few low-ranking members of the Church, where they can easily access information and influence decisions.

- Methods of Influence: The Shrouded use whispers, blackmail, and misinformation to achieve their goals. Members collect secrets on important individuals, hoarding these details for potential leverage. They act as informants and spies, selling information to the highest bidder while concealing their own agendas.
- Creating a Culture of Distrust: The Shrouded spread subtle suspicions, casting doubt on the intentions of community leaders, Church members, and even family members. By fostering paranoia, they weaken the bonds that tie the people of Aru’Mas together, making citizens more likely to distrust one another.
- Encouraging the Hoarding of Knowledge: Followers of Silara often target members of the Order of Wisdom and scholars in general, attempting to corrupt them with the idea that knowledge should not be shared freely. They tempt them to hide discoveries, creating a culture of secrecy and competition in academic and intellectual circles.
- Hidden Shrines in Libraries and Secret Spaces: Shrines to Silara are hidden in places associated with knowledge, like secret rooms in libraries, private study chambers, and even personal archives within influential households. These shrines are often marked with symbols of veils and crescent moons, adorned with parchments bearing cryptic phrases and maps. Followers of Silara visit these shrines to offer their secrets as sacrifices, believing this will grant them further insights into hidden mysteries.

## Goals and Methods

Silara’s ultimate goal is to foster a society where knowledge is hoarded, secrets are valued over transparency, and trust is broken. By encouraging people to keep secrets and hide their intentions, she aims to dismantle the bonds of trust that the Pathite Pantheon promotes, especially the values of openness championed by the Order of Wisdom.

## Strategies to Undermine the Church

- Infiltrating the Order of Wisdom: The Order of Wisdom, which promotes the sharing of knowledge and enlightenment, is Silara’s main target. The Shrouded often infiltrate this Order to subtly undermine its teachings. They may alter records, obscure facts, or add subtle misinformation to documents, corrupting the very knowledge the Order works to preserve.
- Blackmailing Key Figures in Aru’Mas: The Shrouded gather information on powerful individuals, including members of the Church, city officials, and influential merchants. They use this information to manipulate and control these figures, forcing them to act in ways that align with Silara’s goals of secrecy and hidden agendas.
- Spreading Misinformation and Rumors: The Shrouded are masters of disinformation. They plant rumors and twisted truths among the people, creating confusion and doubt regarding the Church’s teachings and the integrity of its leaders. By obscuring the truth, they weaken the Church’s influence over the city and erode the trust people place in the Pathite Pantheon.
- Encouraging Isolation in the Fend: In the Fend, the Shrouded spread the idea that isolation is safer than reliance on others. They encourage families and communities to keep to themselves and avoid outside help, creating a society of secrecy where neighbors grow suspicious of each other’s intentions. This tactic weakens the communal bonds that are crucial to survival in the Fend.

## Silara’s Temptations and Gifts

Silara’s gifts are centered around concealment, control over knowledge, and manipulation. She rewards her followers with abilities that make it easier to gather secrets, conceal their motives, and control the flow of information. Her gifts are especially attractive to those who crave power through influence rather than force.

- Veil of Invisibility: Devout followers of Silara can call upon a “Veil of Invisibility,” allowing them to become unseen for a short period. This gift aids them in gathering information, sneaking into restricted areas, and avoiding detection.
- Whispers of Influence: Silara’s chosen can subtly manipulate those around them through whispered suggestions. By speaking in a low, persuasive tone, they can plant ideas in the minds of others, making them question their own thoughts or intentions.
- Cloak of Secrets: Certain high-ranking followers are granted the Cloak of Secrets, a supernatural ability that allows them to obscure any evidence of their actions, such as erased footprints, disguised handwriting, or altered appearances in the minds of witnesses. This cloak makes it nearly impossible to trace their involvement in any misdeeds.

## Church Countermeasures Against Silara

The Order of Wisdom leads the charge in opposing Silara’s influence, as her values are antithetical to their mission of enlightenment and shared knowledge. The [[Order of Valor]] also assists by uncovering hidden cults and rooting out members of the Shrouded who pose a direct threat to the city’s unity.

- Promoting Transparency and Open Knowledge: The Order of Wisdom regularly holds public lectures, debates, and readings to promote the value of transparency. They work to demystify knowledge and encourage people to seek the truth openly, countering Silara’s influence over secrecy.
- Encouraging Collaboration in Scholarship: Recognizing that Silara’s influence promotes isolation, the Order of Wisdom encourages scholars and intellectuals to work together rather than hoard information. They offer grants and honors for collaborative projects, fostering a spirit of shared discovery that counters the Shrouded’s ideology of secrecy.
- Ritual of Truth’s Light: The Order of Wisdom performs the Ritual of Truth’s Light on a monthly basis, a ceremony in which the city’s citizens are encouraged to confess any lingering doubts, suspicions, or personal secrets. This ritual is seen as a form of purification, freeing participants from the burden of secrecy and reinforcing the importance of honesty.
- The Watchful Eye Initiative: The Order of Valor maintains a special unit known as the Watchful Eye, tasked with tracking down and exposing the Shrouded’s hidden shrines and cult cells. These specialized guards are trained in counter-espionage and are equipped with tools to detect illusion and invisibility, making it harder for Silara’s followers to remain hidden.

## Cultural Myths and Fear of Silara

Silara is seen as a figure who lurks in the shadows of society, her influence felt most strongly in times of secrecy and scandal. Stories and warnings about her are prevalent, especially among those who work with knowledge or information.

- The Tale of the Veiled Scholar: A popular myth tells of a scholar who once devoted himself to Silara, believing that knowledge should be kept only by the worthy. In his quest to learn the world’s secrets, he isolated himself and grew paranoid, afraid that others would steal his knowledge. He eventually went mad, convinced that everyone around him was his enemy. This tale serves as a caution against hoarding knowledge and isolating oneself.
- The “Dark Silence” in the Fend: Fendfolk speak of the “Dark Silence,” a phenomenon in which entire villages are said to fall eerily quiet, with families refusing to speak or leave their homes. Local superstition holds that the Dark Silence is Silara’s doing, her way of ensuring that secrets are kept, even if it means isolating people completely. To ward off her influence, Fendfolk leave offerings of symbolic “truths”—small items or tokens that represent openness and honesty—at the edges of their properties.

## Summary

Silara, the Veil of Secrets, is a deity who thrives on secrecy, isolation, and the control of knowledge. Through her followers, the Shrouded, she seeks to create a society where trust is eroded, knowledge is hoarded, and people live in fear of hidden motives. The Shrouded, operating as spies, informants, and manipulators, work behind the scenes to spread misinformation, blackmail key figures, and isolate communities. Despite Silara’s influence, the Order of Wisdom and Order of Valor strive to counter her effects by promoting transparency, shared knowledge, and open dialogue. Through ceremonies like the Ritual of Truth’s Light and efforts to uncover the Shrouded’s hidden cells, [[The Church of the Threefold Path|the Church of the Threefold Path]] continues to protect the values of trust, unity, and enlightenment from the shadowy grasp of Silara’s influence.
