# Bytopia

Bytopia — the Twin Paradises — is a plane of industrious good, structured as two matching layers of cultivated wilderness and ordered community facing each other across an open sky, each the mirror image of the other. The plane rewards hard work, honest craft, and self-sufficiency; its denizens are neither militaristic nor passive, but purposeful, finding divinity in productive labor and the satisfaction of things well-made.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented relationship. Given Aru'Mas's position as a multiversal convergence point, individuals of Bytopian origin or with Bytopian cultural ties may exist within the city's immigrant population, but no specific connection has been established in current records.
