# Limbo

Limbo is pure chaos without moral alignment — a plane of constant, sourceless transformation where earth becomes fire becomes thought becomes sound without warning or interval, home to slaadi and the githzerai who build their fortress-monasteries in the churning and hold their minds against it. Nothing here is required to remain what it is. That is not a problem Limbo is trying to solve.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented relationship. The [[Primordial Lords]] — particularly [[Vaylis]] and [[Tirilan]], whose domains touch change and paradox — have occasionally been compared to Limbo's nature in theological commentary, but none are believed to have any connection to the plane itself.
