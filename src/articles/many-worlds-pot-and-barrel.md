# Many Worlds Pot and Barrel

In the bustling heart of the [[Far-Farewell Market District]], there’s a tavern unlike any other, drawing crowds with its rich aromas and lively energy. The Many Worlds Pot and Barrel is a vast, open-air tavern known for its warm welcome, abundant seating, and the massive cookpot and grill that lie at its center. This beloved establishment is owned and run by One-Tusk, a friendly, larger-than-life Loxodon chef whose laughter is as hearty as his food. One-Tusk, with his towering frame and big-hearted personality, can often be seen waving and trumpeting a cheerful greeting to patrons as they approach, his trunk raised in a welcoming gesture.

## One-Tusk, the Loxodon Chef

One-Tusk is a legend in the market, known not only for his exceptional cooking but also for his dedication to making every patron feel at home, no matter where they come from. Originally a wandering cook himself, One-Tusk came to Aru’Mas years ago and fell in love with the city’s vibrant energy and diverse crowds. With his thick, well-worn apron and a twinkle in his eye, One-Tusk is as much a storyteller as he is a chef, swapping tales and recipes with patrons from countless worlds. His hands may be enormous, but his touch in the kitchen is surprisingly delicate, and he prides himself on recreating flavors from far and wide with utmost care.

## A Taste of the Many Worlds

At the Pot and Barrel, One-Tusk strives to recreate dishes from across the realms, honoring his patrons’ diverse backgrounds and traditions. He works hard to source ingredients from the different worlds that trade with Aru’Mas, even cultivating relationships with merchants who can bring him hard-to-find spices, herbs, and other key ingredients. As patrons approach, they’re greeted with a lineup of dishes as colorful and varied as the people who visit Aru’Mas. Each meal is prepared with One-Tusk’s own twist—whether it’s a warming stew from the far mountains, a spiced curry from the desert realms, or a tangy seafood dish from a coastal world. For many travelers, a meal here is like a journey home.

## A Lively Gathering Spot

The Pot and Barrel is always full of energy, its tables packed with travelers, adventurers, and locals sharing stories over plates and mugs. The outdoor seating area is strewn with long wooden tables and benches, shaded by colorful canopies and lanterns that glow softly at night, casting a warm, inviting light. Musicians from the market often wander in to play lively tunes, and laughter frequently erupts from all corners of the tavern. Patrons range from merchants to mages, from warriors to wanderers, each sharing a piece of their world over a meal.

## The Signature Cookpot and Grill

At the center of the tavern lies a massive cookpot and open grill, which serves as both the heart of the Pot and Barrel and One-Tusk’s pride and joy. The cookpot is big enough to feed dozens at once, and patrons love to watch as One-Tusk expertly seasons and stirs, his trunk occasionally giving a flourish as he adds spices or checks on a simmering broth. The grill sizzles with skewers of meat, fish, and vegetables, filling the air with mouth-watering aromas. One-Tusk prepares many of his dishes in full view of his customers, taking time to chat, joke, and offer bites to those waiting for their orders.

## The Menu and Specialties

The Pot and Barrel’s menu changes frequently as One-Tusk experiments with new recipes and seasonal ingredients, but there are always a few favorites that keep patrons coming back:

- World’s Edge Stew: A rich, hearty stew made with root vegetables, grains, and tender meat, simmered in a fragrant broth spiced with herbs from the desert realms. A comforting dish for adventurers, it’s said to give strength for the journey ahead.
- Celestial Curry: A spicy, aromatic curry with a deep red color, made from ingredients brought in from far-off, firelit worlds. The curry is served with fluffy rice and a side of soft, warm bread to soak up every drop.
- Sky’s Bounty Skewers: Fresh fish and shellfish marinated in a tangy, citrusy sauce, grilled to perfection and served on long skewers with grilled vegetables. The fish is imported from coastal worlds, making it a rare treat.
- One-Tusk’s Famous Dumplings: Plump, stuffed dumplings filled with savory meats and vegetables, served with dipping sauces from various worlds. Each dumpling has a unique filling inspired by different cultures, a fun way for patrons to sample a bit of everything.

## A Place of Friendship and Connection

The Many Worlds Pot and Barrel is more than just a tavern; it’s a place where strangers become friends, where stories are swapped, and where homesick travelers find a piece of home. One-Tusk’s warm heart and open nature make every visit to the Pot and Barrel feel like a celebration, whether it’s a quiet midday meal or a boisterous dinner surrounded by adventurers from across the realms. For many, the Pot and Barrel is an essential stop, a place that captures the spirit of Aru’Mas itself: welcoming, vibrant, and brimming with the flavors of many worlds.
