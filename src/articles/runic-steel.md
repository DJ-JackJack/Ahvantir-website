# Runic Steel

Runic Steel is a rare, naturally occurring metal found only in Ahvantir, where ley lines converge and ancient spiritual energies infuse the land itself. Formed over centuries in areas of intense magic, this metal is imbued with a unique ability to both enhance magic and resist hostile enchantments.

## Properties of Runic Steel

Runic Steel is celebrated for its exceptional durability, magic-conducting abilities, and defensive resistance against external enchantments. These properties reflect the dual nature of Ahvantir’s spiritual magic: harmonious with friendly intent, yet resistant to hostile interference.

## 1. Resilience and Durability

- Enhanced Hardness: Runic Steel is much harder and more durable than traditional metals due to the ancient magical infusion it received over centuries. Items made from Runic Steel are immune to wear and tear, often resisting non-magical damage, which makes them ideal for constant use.
- Increased Lifespan: The natural resilience of Runic Steel makes it resistant to corrosion and rust, ensuring that weapons and armor crafted from it last much longer than their mundane counterparts. This durability is highly valued by adventurers and protectors of Ahvantir.

## 2. Magical Conduction and Enhancement

Runic Steel has a unique sympathy to the wielder’s intent, allowing it to conduct and amplify magic when aligned with its wielder’s purpose.

- Innate Magic Attunement: Runic Steel channels spells and enchantments more effectively than typical materials. This capacity is prized by those who need versatility and adaptability in battle.
- Enhanced Spell Conduction: When used willingly by a spellcaster or magic user, Runic Steel offers a +1 bonus to spell attack rolls while wielding Runic Steel weapons. This property makes it particularly valuable for casters and spiritualists who seek harmony with the metal’s natural magic.

## 3. Natural Rune-Like Effects

Runic Steel possesses faint, organic patterns that resemble runes, believed to be a result of the earth’s natural magic. These patterns grant the metal special effects when crafted into tools, weapons, or armor.

- Rune of Striking: Weapons crafted from Runic Steel deal an additional 1d4 radiant or force damage per strike, enhancing the wielder’s attacks with the magic resonating within the metal. This effect is highly favored by the [[Runeguard]] and other protectors.
- Rune of Warding: Armor or shields bearing this natural effect provide resistance to a specific damage type (chosen at the time of forging), such as fire or lightning, giving wearers protection against certain elemental threats.

## 4. Anti-Magic Resistance

While Runic Steel enhances magic willingly used by its wielder, it has a natural resistance to hostile enchantments.

- Resistance to Enchantment: Armor or jewelry crafted from Runic Steel grants the wearer advantage on saving throws against being charmed or magically influenced, making it invaluable for those who face mental manipulation. This property manifests as a natural defense, repelling attempts to exert magical control over its wielder.

## Explanation of Dual Properties

Runic Steel’s ability to both enhance and resist magic is rooted in its connection to Ahvantir’s ancient forces. Its magical conductivity reflects its receptiveness to friendly magic, aligning with the wielder’s intent and conducting magic freely. However, when hostile magic is applied, Runic Steel’s nature as a guardian metal resists this interference, acting as a barrier against unwanted enchantments.

## Sourcing Runic Steel

Runic Steel is found only in select areas within Ahvantir, often in ancient, powerful places where ley lines intersect or where spirits have long inhabited the land. These veins are highly protected by spirits, who view the metal as a part of Ahvantir’s essence. Mining Runic Steel without permission or reverence can incur spiritual retribution or curses from local Wardens.

## Crafting and Shaping Runic Steel

Working with Runic Steel requires the guidance of a skilled blacksmith and a spiritual expert who understands its natural patterns and dual nature. Unlike other metals, Runic Steel must be worked with reverence and care, as its natural magic aligns only with those who honor it. Mishandling the metal or forcing it into forms contrary to its nature can result in bad luck or curses.

Most Runic Steel items are heirlooms, passed down through generations or awarded to guardians and heroes of Ahvantir. These items are symbols of trust and respect, given to those who understand and embody the spiritual harmony of the land.
