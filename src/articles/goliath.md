# Goliath

The Goliath is the largest and most formidable vessel of the [[Starleaper Fleet]], designed to carry immense cargo and provide unparalleled defense. A marvel of engineering and arcane craftsmanship, the Goliath is a mobile stronghold, capable of weathering the most dangerous environments the multiverse has to offer. Its primary function is to transport large shipments of goods for trade or delivery, but its secondary—and equally vital—role is to evacuate refugees from endangered worlds, offering them safe passage to Aru’Mas.

## Design and Features

## Immense Size and Capacity

The Goliath’s massive size dwarfs the other ships in the Fleet, allowing it to carry vast quantities of cargo or large numbers of passengers. Its lower decks are dedicated to storage, designed to hold everything from trade goods to food supplies, with modular compartments that can be rearranged to accommodate refugees and their belongings.

## Defensive Capabilities

The Goliath is outfitted with state-of-the-art defensive measures, making it the most resilient ship in the Fleet:

- Reinforced Hull: Crafted from [[Runic Steel]] and enchanted with layers of protective wards, the hull can withstand astral storms, hostile attacks, and even direct impacts from celestial debris.
- Barrier Shields: The ship’s magical barrier can repel energy-based and physical assaults, ensuring the safety of its crew and passengers.
- Arcane Turrets: While not primarily a combat vessel, the Goliath is equipped with defensive turrets capable of firing bursts of concentrated magical energy to deter attackers or clear obstacles in its path.

## Slow but Steady

The Goliath’s immense size and heavy defensive measures make it significantly slower than the other ships in the Starleaper Fleet. However, its strength and dependability compensate for its lack of speed. The ship is known for being a steady presence in chaotic situations, able to hold its ground against nearly any threat.

## Accommodations for Refugees

In addition to its cargo holds, the Goliath features specialized living quarters for displaced individuals. These quarters are simple but comfortable, with enough space to provide dignity and safety for hundreds of people. Enchanted kitchens and medical bays ensure that basic needs are met during long journeys, while communal areas offer space for rest and recovery.

## Primary Roles and Missions

## 1. Cargo Transport

The Goliath is the backbone of the Starleaper Fleet’s trade operations, hauling goods between Aru’Mas and its multiversal allies. Its immense capacity allows it to carry a wide variety of items, including:

- Raw Materials: Rare metals, magical components, and resources vital to Aru’Mas’s economy.
- Cultural Artifacts: Unique crafts and goods from distant worlds, enriching the city’s markets and museums.
- Humanitarian Supplies: Food, medicine, and tools for communities in need, often delivered to allies or struggling worlds.

## 2. Refugee Evacuation

Perhaps the Goliath’s most noble role is its use as a lifeboat for endangered populations. When a world faces destruction—whether from natural disaster, war, or planar collapse—the Goliath is dispatched to evacuate as many people as possible. The ship’s immense capacity and robust defenses make it ideal for navigating hostile environments and providing sanctuary to those in need.

The Goliath has become a symbol of hope for countless worlds, its arrival signaling salvation for those who believed they had no way out. Crews are trained not only in logistics and defense but also in providing comfort and support to traumatized refugees.

## Crews and Command

## Leadership and Crew

The Goliath’s size and complexity require a large and highly skilled crew. Key personnel include:

- The Captain: A seasoned leader chosen for their ability to remain calm under pressure and manage both logistical and humanitarian challenges.
- Quartermasters: Specialists in cargo management who ensure that space is used efficiently and that resources are distributed equitably during evacuation missions.
- Defenders: Trained warriors and mages responsible for protecting the ship and its passengers.
- Medics and Counselors: Crewmembers skilled in healing magic and emotional support, providing care to those rescued by the Goliath.

## Teamwork and Coordination

The crew of the Goliath works closely with other ships in the Fleet, often serving as the central hub during large-scale operations. Smaller ships like the [[Sunspear]] are sometimes used to scout or clear a path for the Goliath, ensuring its safe passage.

## Legends and Reputation

The Goliath is celebrated across the multiverse as a beacon of resilience and compassion. Its legendary reliability and critical role in humanitarian efforts have inspired countless tales and songs:

## The Unbreakable Fortress

In one famous incident, the Goliath was caught in the heart of an astral storm while carrying refugees from a collapsing realm. Despite being bombarded by shards of astral debris and lashed by chaotic energies, the ship held firm, its shields and hull enduring long enough to deliver its passengers to safety. This event earned the Goliath the nickname “The Unbreakable Fortress.”

## The Silent Savior

In another tale, the Goliath rescued thousands of refugees from a war-torn world under siege by planar invaders. Its crew worked tirelessly to evacuate as many people as possible, even under constant attack. The ship’s defensive systems repelled every assault, and it left the battlefield unscathed, earning the gratitude of an entire civilization.

## Cultural Significance

The Goliath holds a unique place in the hearts of the people of Aru’Mas and the worlds it has aided. Its dual role as a cargo vessel and a lifeline for refugees makes it a symbol of the city’s commitment to both commerce and compassion.

## In Aru’Mas

While many citizens of Aru’Mas see the Goliath as a distant figure, its humanitarian missions are a point of pride. The ship’s efforts reflect the city’s values, reinforcing its identity as a place where courage, resourcefulness, and generosity come together.

## Across the Multiverse

For those in distant worlds, the Goliath is often the first—and sometimes the only—encounter with Aru’Mas. Its reputation as a savior has spread far and wide, inspiring respect and gratitude from countless communities.

## Rumors and Mysteries

As with any vessel of such importance, the Goliath is surrounded by rumors and speculation:

- Hidden Weapons: Some claim the Goliath has secret weaponry capable of devastating entire fleets, though no evidence supports this theory.
- An Ever-Growing Heart: Legend has it that the ship’s cargo hold is enchanted to expand infinitely, allowing it to carry as many passengers or goods as needed. While likely an exaggeration, the ship’s capacity does seem to defy conventional limits.
- The Warden’s Blessing: It is whispered that a powerful spirit, perhaps even [[Yan Bo Tog]] himself, watches over the Goliath, ensuring its safety in the Astral Sea.
