# [[Order of Harmony]] Hospitals

The hospitals of the Order of Harmony serve as vital lifelines across [[Aru'Mas]], offering free healing and basic healthcare to all, regardless of social standing. Established under the guiding principles of compassion and service, the Order's healthcare network spans the city's districts, ensuring that even the poorest residents receive some level of medical attention. However, despite their noble mission, the disparities between hospitals in affluent districts and those in poorer neighborhoods have led to significant criticism.

## Structure and Function

The Order of Harmony's healthcare system consists of numerous hospitals and clinics spread throughout the city's districts, each varying in size, staffing, and available resources. These facilities are primarily funded through donations, support from noble patrons, and limited government assistance. While the Order remains committed to providing free medical care, the reality of economic disparity results in significant differences in the level of treatment available between wealthier and poorer districts. The hospitals are staffed by clerics, healers, alchemists, and lay practitioners, who work tirelessly to provide treatments ranging from mundane first aid to advanced magical healing. Facilities in richer districts enjoy cutting-edge medical advancements, while those in impoverished areas rely on makeshift solutions and overworked staff.

## Disparities in Healthcare

## Hospitals in Affluent Districts (e.g., The [[Luminous Reach]], [[Spireview]])

- Resources and Staffing: These hospitals are equipped with advanced medical supplies, enchanted healing tools, and an abundance of clerics and trained healers. They are often supported by substantial donations from wealthy patrons, enabling the purchase of rare herbs and runic healing implements.
- Facilities: Spacious, well-maintained, and aesthetically pleasing, these hospitals boast private rooms, comfortable recovery areas, and additional amenities such as libraries and gardens for convalescence.
- Services Offered: Beyond basic healthcare, these hospitals provide access to advanced magical treatments, long-term care for chronic conditions, and specialized healing techniques.
- Public Perception: Residents of these districts often view the Order of Harmony favorably, as their local hospitals exemplify efficiency and excellence in care.

## Hospitals in Impoverished Districts (e.g., [[The Spillway]], [[Driftmoor Haven]])

- Resources and Staffing: These hospitals are often understaffed, with fewer clerics and healers managing a higher patient load. Supplies are limited, and the staff frequently resorts to improvising treatments due to the lack of high-quality materials.
- Facilities: Cramped and minimally maintained, these hospitals may lack privacy for patients and adequate spaces for recovery. Some operate out of repurposed buildings or shared spaces.
- Services Offered: While basic healing and care are provided, magical treatments are rare. Chronic conditions or complex cases require referral to better-equipped hospitals in wealthier districts, which many cannot afford or access.
- Public Perception: These hospitals are both a blessing and a source of frustration. While they provide essential services, their limited capacity and perceived neglect contribute to resentment toward the Order of Harmony.

## Efforts to Bridge the Gap

The Order of Harmony encourages donations to improve its services, and some affluent districts organize fundraising drives to support hospitals in poorer neighborhoods. However, critics argue that systemic changes are needed to ensure equitable healthcare across Aru'Mas. Proposals include:

- Reallocating resources to underfunded hospitals.
- Expanding training for healers in underprivileged areas.
- Increasing accountability within the Order to ensure fair distribution of aid.

The disparity between hospitals highlights the broader economic inequalities within Aru'Mas, making healthcare a central issue in discussions about social reform in the city.

## Hospitals by District

## The Luminous Reach

The hospital here is a grand, well-staffed medical center known for its luxurious private rooms and access to the most powerful magical healing in the city. Specialized wings treat rare diseases, mental health conditions, and long-term care needs.

## Spireview

A center of medical innovation, this hospital works closely with magical researchers to develop new healing techniques. It offers cutting-edge experimental treatments, making it sought after by those with chronic or untreatable ailments.

## The [[Crowns Approach]]

This secure hospital caters to high-ranking government officials and foreign dignitaries, ensuring absolute discretion and the highest level of care. Its staff is trained in treating poisons and exotic illnesses from foreign lands.

## [[Hearthstone]]

A well-rounded hospital catering to the middle class, balancing magical and non-magical medicine. This facility also serves as a training ground for new healers, making it a critical part of the city’s healthcare system.

## [[Stonegate]]

Given its proximity to the [[Spiritsway Passage]], this hospital is one of the best at treating afflictions caused by spirits and supernatural forces. Healers here are trained in advanced spiritual warding and restoration techniques.

## [[Temple Way]]

This hospital is unique in that it receives aid from multiple religious groups, making it one of the most diverse in healing practices. It specializes in divine healing, curse removals, and faith-based therapy.

## The [[Aurora Song District]]

Here, the hospital embraces creative and holistic healing, offering music therapy, enchanted art, and alternative magical practices. Many who suffer from stress-related or mental health issues seek treatment here.

## Driftmoor Haven

This facility struggles with overcrowding but remains essential to the working-class community. It specializes in treating labor-related injuries, minor illnesses, and emergency cases for sailors and dockworkers.

## The Spillway

A crumbling structure with inadequate resources, this hospital does its best with what little it has. The Order relies on volunteers and traveling healers to keep it operational.

## [[Ragmarket]]

The district lacks a proper hospital, with only makeshift clinics staffed by underground medics and alchemists. Many treatments here come at a cost, requiring barter or alternative payments.

## Conclusion

The state of healthcare across Aru’Mas remains a pressing issue, highlighting the economic divides that shape life in the city. While the Order of Harmony works tirelessly to provide healing for all, the question remains whether they will ever truly bridge the gap between the privileged and the struggling masses.
