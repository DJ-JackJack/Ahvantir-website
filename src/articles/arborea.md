# Arborea

Arborea is a plane of passionate good — forests large enough to be worlds unto themselves, wine-dark seas, and mountains whose peaks are the thrones of divine presences, all suffused with an emotional intensity that transforms even ordinary acts into something that feels mythic. It is chaotic and good, generous to the point of excess, and its beauty is inseparable from its violence.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented relationship. The emotional lordship described in accounts of Arborea — divine figures embodying single, overwhelming passions — has occasionally prompted scholarly comparison with the Lords and Ladies of Emotion who govern the [[Vast Green]], but the two planes are understood as distinct and no connection has been established.
