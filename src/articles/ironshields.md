# Ironshields

The Ironshields are the elite heavy infantry of the [[Sunspear Legion]] , tasked with defending the fortified heart of Aru’Mas and its critical infrastructure. These warriors serve as the unbreakable bulwark of the city’s defenses, renowned for their ability to withstand direct assaults, secure key positions, and hold the line even in the face of overwhelming odds. As the embodiment of the Legion’s values of strength, discipline, and unyielding resolve, the Ironshields are one of the most respected units within the military forces of Aru’Mas.

## Role and Purpose

The Ironshields are the backbone of the city’s ground defense, fulfilling several critical roles in maintaining security, law enforcement, and control during conflicts:

## 1. Defensive Stronghold Forces

- The Ironshields are primarily stationed at the most important locations within Aru’Mas, including the [[Heartspire]], the Council Hall, and the [[Runeguard]] Citadel. They are tasked with fortifying these areas against spiritual incursions, enemy sieges, and multiversal breaches.
- They serve as the last line of defense during sieges or large-scale assaults, using their heavy armor and shields to protect the most vulnerable positions, including magical wards, defensive runes, and key personnel.

## 2. Law Enforcement and Riot Control

- The Ironshields are deployed during times of civil unrest or large public gatherings, providing both visible security and the muscle needed to maintain order. With their imposing presence and disciplined formations, they can disperse crowds, establish barricades, and break up conflicts without resorting to excessive violence.
- They are also responsible for enforcing martial law when necessary, working in coordination with the Runeguard and the city’s judiciary to apprehend dangerous individuals and maintain peace during emergencies.

## 3. Siege Warfare Specialists

- Ironshields are trained in both offensive and defensive siege tactics, capable of manning battlements, operating siege weaponry, and repelling assaults from both ground and aerial enemies. They excel at fortifying strongholds and reinforcing critical positions with both physical barriers and arcane wards.
- During offensive operations, Ironshields serve as the vanguard, using their heavy armor and shields to push through enemy lines, break fortified positions, and establish beachheads for the rest of the Legion.

## Ironshield Equipment and Combat Tactics

The Ironshields are defined by their distinctive, heavily fortified armor, powerful weapons, and coordinated combat tactics, which emphasize discipline and durability.

## 1. Heavy Armor

- Ironshields wear arcane-enhanced plate armor, crafted from runesteel, a rare, enchanted metal that is both lightweight and incredibly durable. Each piece is inscribed with warding runes that offer protection against physical attacks and magical effects, including fireballs, curses, and spirit-based assaults.
- The armor is designed to be modular, allowing for quick repairs and modifications based on the mission. It includes components like reinforced pauldrons, layered greaves, and a closed helm that can be adjusted for enhanced arcane vision.

## 2. Tower Shields

- The iconic weapon of the Ironshields is their tower shield, a massive, rectangular shield that stands nearly as tall as its wielder. These shields are enchanted with defensive runes that can generate temporary barriers or reflect spells back at attackers.
- In battle, Ironshields use their tower shields to form an impenetrable wall, creating a defensive front that can absorb enemy charges, arrows, and spells. This tactic is central to their role as fortified infantry, enabling them to hold key positions even under intense pressure.

## 3. Mauls and Warhammers

- In addition to their shields, Ironshields carry heavy mauls or warhammers designed for close-quarters combat. These weapons are enchanted with runes of impact and shattering, allowing them to crush armor, break bones, and even breach magical barriers with a single, devastating blow.
- Ironshields are trained to use their mauls and warhammers in tandem with their shields, maximizing both offense and defense in tight formations. The combination of heavy armor, impenetrable shields, and crushing weapons makes them formidable opponents in any melee encounter.

## Training and Organization

Ironshields undergo one of the most grueling training regimens in the [[Sunspear Legion]] , designed to build both physical endurance and unyielding resolve.

## 1. Recruitment and Selection

- Only the strongest and most resilient warriors are chosen to join the Ironshields. Recruits must pass rigorous physical tests, including prolonged endurance runs, shield wall drills, and weapon mastery exercises.
- Prospective Ironshields are tested in mental fortitude as well, undergoing scenarios designed to simulate the pressures of holding the line against overwhelming odds. This includes being outnumbered in combat drills, facing relentless spiritual attacks, and enduring extended sieges without rest.

## 2. Training Regimen

- Ironshields train extensively in shield wall tactics, perfecting the art of moving as a single, unified formation. This includes drills on rotating shield-bearers, reinforcing defensive lines, and creating overlapping fields of defense with their shields and armor.
- Combat training emphasizes close-quarters fighting, with a focus on teamwork and maintaining cohesion in the heat of battle. Ironshields learn to deliver synchronized strikes, coordinate defensive maneuvers, and exploit enemy weaknesses during prolonged engagements.

## 3. Structure and Command

- The Ironshields are led by the Shieldmaster, currently Shieldmaster Garren Vex, a battle-hardened veteran known for his unyielding presence on the battlefield and strategic brilliance. Garren earned his rank after leading a heroic defense during the Siege of Runeguard Citadel, where he held the line against a massive spirit attack for several hours.
- The Ironshields are divided into phalanxes, each led by a Phalanx Captain who commands a unit of 20-30 Ironshields. Each phalanx is trained to operate as a self-sufficient unit, capable of establishing defensive positions and repelling attacks on its own.
- Within the phalanxes, the Ironshields also organize into smaller units called Bastions, each consisting of 5-10 soldiers who specialize in specific roles, such as siege-breaking, shield reinforcement, or magical suppression.

## Missions and Duties

The Ironshields are involved in various missions that highlight their role as stalwart defenders of Aru’Mas:

## 1. Guarding Key Locations

- The Ironshields are stationed at the most important locations in the city, including the Heartspire, the Council Hall, and the Runeguard Citadel. They are responsible for protecting these areas from spiritual incursions, magical attacks, and physical sieges.
- Their presence at these locations is a visible symbol of Aru’Mas’ strength, reinforcing the sense of security among the city’s inhabitants and government officials.

## 2. Suppressing Spiritual Incursions

- When spiritual breaches occur within the city, the Ironshields are often deployed to suppress rogue spirits, erect temporary barriers, and protect the citizens. Their armor is specially warded against spiritual attacks, making them effective at holding the line until the Runeguard or the [[Order of Harmony]] can neutralize the threat.
- The Ironshields’ unflinching discipline is vital in such situations, as they must maintain formation and focus even when facing spirits that use fear, confusion, or curses to disrupt their defenses.

## 3. City Patrols and Crowd Control

- The Ironshields perform patrols across the city, maintaining law and order in high-risk areas and responding to emergencies that require a show of force. Their presence is often enough to quell potential riots or conflicts, as few wish to challenge their sheer power.
- During major public events, they are deployed for crowd control, using their shields to create barriers, establish safe zones, and ensure that crowds remain orderly.

## Reputation and Public Perception

The Ironshields are viewed as both heroes and guardians by the citizens of Aru’Mas, representing the city’s unyielding strength and stability.

## 1. Admiration and Respect

- Citizens admire the Ironshields for their dedication and bravery in defending the city, often seeing them as a symbol of protection. The Ironshields’ ability to hold the line in the face of overwhelming odds is a source of pride and reassurance for the people of Aru’Mas.
- Their role in suppressing spiritual incursions and defending the city’s most important locations has made them widely respected, even among the adventurers’ guilds, who view them as essential allies.

## 2. Awe and Fear

- The sheer size and power of the Ironshields can be intimidating, especially for newcomers or visitors from other realms. Their heavy armor and unyielding demeanor create an aura of authority that deters most from challenging them.
- The Ironshields are often seen as the ultimate enforcers of law and order, making them both revered and feared by those who cross into the darker sides of Aru’Mas’ politics or multiversal dealings.
