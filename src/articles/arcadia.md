# Arcadia

Arcadia is a plane of ordered good — harmonious, productive, and beautiful in a measured way that tends toward community rather than hierarchy. Its fields yield abundantly, its settlements operate with the efficiency of things that have been doing what they do for a very long time, and its constant, unvarying light gives the plane an atmosphere of gentle and permanent reliability that visitors either find deeply comforting or slightly uncanny.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented relationship. No Ahvantir deity, order, or significant figure has claimed Arcadia as a relevant domain in any current record.
