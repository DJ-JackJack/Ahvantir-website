# Mount Celestia

Mount Celestia — formally the Seven Mounting Heavens of Celestia — is a plane of absolute law and good, structured as seven heavenly mountains stacked one atop the other, each layer more refined and more unbearably luminous than the last. It is home to archons, angels, and the divine servants of lawful good deities, and its denizens are among the few outer-planar entities known to cross into mortal worlds in times of cosmic significance.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented relationship. The disciplined, hierarchical good embodied by Mount Celestia's archons is sometimes invoked in [[The Church of the Threefold Path|Threefold Path]] sermons as an ideal of law wedded to virtue, but this is rhetorical comparison rather than evidence of any contact between the plane and Ahvantir.
