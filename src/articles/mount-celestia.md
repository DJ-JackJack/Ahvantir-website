# Mount Celestia

Mount Celestia — formally the Seven Mounting Heavens of Celestia — is a plane of absolute law and good, structured as seven heavenly mountains stacked one atop the other, each layer more refined and more unbearably luminous than the last. It is home to archons, angels, and the divine servants of lawful good deities, and its denizens are among the few outer-planar entities known to cross into mortal worlds in times of cosmic significance.

**Ahvantir connection:** The cast-down Archangel [[Asgoraviel]] — who besieged Aru'Mas during the Siege of Asgoraviel (~330–339 MC) and was subsequently sealed beneath the [[Heartspire]] — was a celestial entity believed to be of Mount Celestian origin, stripped of standing in the higher planes before their assault on the city. This remains the only documented instance of direct contact between Mount Celestia's hierarchy and Ahvantir's mortal history. Whether other Celestian entities have since taken interest in Ahvantir is not established.
