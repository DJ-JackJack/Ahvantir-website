# Radiant Moonglow

Radiant Moonglow is a rare, mystical liquid that appears only during the rare alignment of Ahvantir’s three moons—Miras, Toris, and Keltas. This enchanting substance seems to seep up from the earth itself, as if drawn out by the unique lunar energies, and remains collectible for only an hour under the tri-moon alignment. The liquid has a translucent lilac hue, and in the moonlight, it glimmers with an otherworldly light, as though infused with stardust. Radiant Moonglow is highly prized for its legendary healing properties, which are rumored to surpass those of any known potion or magic.

## Properties of Radiant Moonglow

Radiant Moonglow is both a healing balm and a powerful spiritual conduit, making it invaluable to healers, alchemists, and spiritualists across Ahvantir. However, its collection and use require expertise, reverence, and careful preparation.

## 1. Enhanced Healing Powers

- Potent Regeneration: When ingested or applied, Radiant Moonglow promotes rapid regeneration and healing of wounds, with effects ranging from instantaneous healing of injuries to recovery from diseases that would typically require prolonged magical intervention. Even those close to death can be restored to vitality if treated with Moonglow in time.
- Restoration of Limbs and Senses: In concentrated form, Radiant Moonglow is rumored to regenerate lost limbs, restore sight to the blind, and hearing to the deaf. However, such applications require a high quantity of Moonglow and special preparation by skilled healers or alchemists.
- Extended Lifespan: There are tales that Radiant Moonglow, when taken in very small, carefully diluted doses over time, can extend the lifespan of the drinker by years or even decades. Though rare and difficult to confirm, it’s said that some of Ahvantir’s oldest healers have used Moonglow to preserve their health well beyond typical mortal limits.

## 2. Spiritual and Mystical Affinities

- Spiritual Conduit: Radiant Moonglow is as much a spiritual substance as it is a physical one. It’s considered a sacred elixir capable of enhancing the user’s connection to Ahvantir’s spirits. When used in rituals, Moonglow serves as a bridge between worlds, helping mortals communicate with or channel the spirits of the land.
- Empowerment of Divination and Spiritual Magic: Radiant Moonglow’s lunar origin makes it particularly potent for divination spells, ritual magic, and spiritual attunements. Some spiritualists believe that consuming or anointing oneself with Moonglow before entering a spirit’s domain makes one more attuned to the realm’s energies, offering heightened sensitivity to the Old Pacts and allowing clearer communication with spirits.
- Necromantic Reversal (Rare Legend): According to ancient folklore, Radiant Moonglow holds the potential to bring back the dead when combined with other rare ingredients—such as sacred herbs from a Locus, a vial of [[Runic Steel]] essence, or a fragment of [[Rootrot Crystal]] (in rare, dark rituals). While such feats are rarely attempted and dangerous, Moonglow’s unique energies theoretically hold the potential to restore a soul to the body, making it one of the few known materials in Ahvantir associated with resurrection.

## Collection and Usage of Radiant Moonglow

Due to its rarity and spiritual potency, collecting and using Radiant Moonglow is considered a sacred practice.

## 1. Ritual of Collection

- Tri-Moon Alignment: Radiant Moonglow appears only during the alignment of Miras, Toris, and Keltas, which occurs only once every few years. The alignment draws out lunar energies from deep within the land, causing the Moonglow to seep up through cracks in the ground or pool in natural basins.
- Ritual Preparations: Collectors typically conduct a ritual of cleansing and reverence before setting out, seeking blessings from the spirits to avoid disturbing the land’s balance. Gathering Moonglow requires specific vessels—typically silver-lined containers or glass vials etched with protective runes—as it will evaporate or lose potency if stored improperly.
- Hour of Collection: Moonglow is only collectible for a brief window during the tri-moon alignment, often no more than an hour. Collectors must work quickly and carefully, as mishandling or disrespecting the process can result in bad luck or spiritual repercussions.

## 2. Preparation for Use

- Dilution for Potency Control: Radiant Moonglow is often diluted with pure water or natural spring essence to reduce its potency to a safe level, especially when consumed. Raw, undiluted Moonglow can be too potent for most mortals to handle, resulting in severe exhaustion, heightened sensitivity to lunar cycles, or in rare cases, visions or uncontrollable weeping.
- Blending with Other Materials: Alchemists experiment with mixing Moonglow with other rare materials to heighten or channel its properties. For example, combining it with herbs from the [[Neverwood]] creates powerful healing salves, while mixing it with Runic Steel essence is said to enhance spells tied to protection, dreams, and fate.

## 3. Spiritual and Cultural Reverence

- Sacred Status: Radiant Moonglow is regarded with the utmost reverence in Ahvantir, considered a gift from the moons and a blessing of life. Its appearance is seen as a time of celebration and spiritual reflection, often marked by rituals and gatherings at sacred sites where Moonglow is known to emerge.
- Guardianship and Custodians: Those who collect and store Moonglow are often appointed by local spiritual leaders and undergo extensive training in purification and ritual handling. These custodians hold a respected position in society, and only those with deep knowledge of the spiritual world are trusted to handle Moonglow safely.
