# House Chromewell

The Verdant Wardens of [[Aru'Mas]]

Motto: “In Harmony, We Endure”Species: ElvesDistrict Influence: [[Temple Way]], SpireviewAllied Organizations: [[Order of the Keystone]], [[Order of Wisdom]]

## Overview

One of the six original noble houses of Aru'Mas, House Chromewell represents a deep and abiding commitment to the natural and spiritual balance of Ahvantir. Their elven lineage stems from a companion of [[Marduk Sunspear]] who first brokered peace with the island’s oldest spirits, establishing the foundations for the city’s famed Old Pacts.

Often called the Verdant Wardens, Chromewell’s influence reaches into matters of spiritual diplomacy, environmental stewardship, and arcane ecology. Members of the house frequently serve as druids, mediators, and emissaries to powerful spirits that dwell in and around Aru’Mas.

## Political Role

In Council deliberations, House Chromewell is a quiet but significant force. They speak rarely, choosing their battles with care, but when spirit law or ecological stability is threatened, their intervention can halt major legislation. Their votes often align with the Order of Wisdom, and their guidance is sought in treaty discussions that involve extraplanar or spiritual territories.

The house is also a key voice in resisting unchecked urban expansion and has opposed multiple Cryxis-backed infrastructure projects they believe would disrupt pact-bound lands.

## Reputation and Public Perception

To many citizens, House Chromewell feels like a relic of an older Aru'Mas. Their serene detachment and refusal to compromise core values can make them seem aloof or elitist. Yet in times of spiritual unrest—when spirits stir, or pacts are tested—it is often the Chromewells who are called upon to restore harmony.

They are deeply respected in districts like Temple Way, where their ancestral ties to the spirit realms are a source of quiet reverence. However, their limited presence in working-class areas makes them feel distant to many commonfolk.

## Cultural Traditions

House Chromewell practices a blend of elven ritualism and pact-bound ceremony, with each family member expected to form and maintain a personal spirit relationship before adulthood. Sacred groves and ritual stones dot their holdings, and their internal governance often defers to omens or spirit counsel.

They consider silence a sign of strength and reflection, often conducting internal matters in near-complete quiet save for natural sounds. Chromewell garments favor living fibers—woven moss, enchanted silkvine, and reflective petals—that change subtly with the seasons.

## Legacy and Holdings

Their primary estate sits on the edge of Temple Way, partially fused with an ancient grove that predates Aru'Mas itself. Rather than expanding outward, the estate deepens downward and inward—into spirit-glades, root-chambers, and pact sanctums protected by living wards.

In [[Spireview]], they maintain a quiet spire that functions as both a library and diplomatic station for meetings with extraplanar envoys. Their holdings are modest in scale but rich in spiritual potency and natural integration.

## Notable Members

- Thalendriel Chromewell – Current house leader and representative to the Council. Reserved and deeply spiritual, he is rarely seen outside grove sanctuaries.
- Maelira Chromewell – Senior voice in the Order of the Keystone. She is respected for her measured tone and decades of pact mediation.
- Elizabeth Chromewell – Estranged granddaughter of the house. A skilled herbalist and healer, quietly tolerated in Spireview, though never formally acknowledged as an heir.

## Cultural Symbolism

The Chromewell crest features a silver tree with roots tangled in a knot of sky blue and moss green—representing balance between spirit and soil. Their house colors—moss green, slate silver, and sky blue—evoke natural serenity and quiet wisdom.
