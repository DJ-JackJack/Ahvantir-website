# Yarudvic

Yarudvic is a unique and enigmatic figure among the [[Usurpers]] of Ahvantir. Hailing from one of the countless layers of the Abyss, Yarudvic has defied expectations of his kind by establishing himself as a benevolent, if unsettling, presence within the city of Aru’Mas. His Claim, The Last Chance, is a sprawling, otherworldly inn that appears unassuming from the outside but contains over 100 rooms, each imbued with subtle magic designed to ease pain and nurture healing.

While Yarudvic thrives on the negative emotions of those who reside within his Claim, he does not provoke or encourage these feelings; rather, he feeds on what already exists, offering shelter and comfort in return. His methods, though unconventional and sometimes misunderstood, have made him a quiet yet invaluable ally to the city’s most vulnerable.

## Yarudvic’s Appearance and Presence

Yarudvic’s true form is rarely seen, but his presence is unmistakable.

- Appearance: Yarudvic typically takes the form of a tall, elegant humanoid with sharp, angular features, crimson skin, and curling black horns. His eyes glow faintly gold, a disconcerting but oddly soothing contrast to his demonic heritage. He often dresses in finely tailored black and silver robes, exuding an air of calm authority.
- Aura of Dread and Comfort: Despite his demonic origins, Yarudvic carries an aura that is simultaneously unnerving and reassuring. Those in his presence feel their fears and traumas surface, but rather than overwhelming them, these emotions feel manageable, as though guided toward resolution.

## The Last Chance

Yarudvic’s Claim, The Last Chance, is as much a reflection of his character as it is a manifestation of his power.

- Physical Appearance: From the outside, the inn is a modest, weathered building nestled on the edge of the Temple District and the Far-Farewell Market. A simple wooden sign bearing the name "The Last Chance" swings above its door. Inside, however, the inn expands into a vast, labyrinthine space with over 100 rooms, each tailored to the needs of its occupant.
- Magical Properties:
- Emotionally Adaptive Rooms: Each room subtly shifts to reflect and soothe the occupant’s emotional state. A grieving widow might find herself in a room filled with soft light and the scent of lavender, while a traumatized soldier might see a battlefield replaced with a serene meadow.
- The Hearth of Solace: The inn’s common room features an ever-burning hearth, the flames of which exude a calming warmth that seems to dull even the sharpest of pains.
- Boundaries of the Claim: Yarudvic’s influence is absolute within the Last Chance. Violence, theft, or malicious acts are impossible, as the inn’s very walls seem to absorb such impulses.

## Yarudvic’s Role in Aru’Mas

Yarudvic occupies a unique niche within the city, straddling the line between feared outsider and cherished protector.

## 1. A Safe Haven for the Broken

The Last Chance is renowned as a sanctuary for those who have nowhere else to turn. Yarudvic personally welcomes wounded soldiers, grieving widows, exiled criminals seeking redemption, and anyone burdened by fear or sorrow. He offers no judgment, only shelter, and asks nothing in return but the emotions he feeds upon.

## 2. A Mediator and Counselor

Yarudvic possesses an uncanny ability to read emotions and discern the root causes of pain. He often acts as a counselor, guiding his guests through their struggles with gentle but firm wisdom. Some say his insight comes from centuries of torment and cruelty in the Abyss, while others believe it is the mark of a spirit truly seeking redemption.

## 3. The City’s Shadow Healer

Though not affiliated with the [[Order of Harmony]], Yarudvic has become an unofficial figure of healing within Aru’Mas. While the Order offers physical and spiritual care, Yarudvic addresses the wounds of the heart and mind. His methods are unorthodox, but many who leave the Last Chance find themselves lighter and more at peace.

## Rumors and Stories of Yarudvic

## A Demonic Past

Legends abound that Yarudvic was once a brutal demon prince, infamous for his cruelty and insatiable hunger for suffering. Some say he was cast out of the Abyss for unknown reasons, while others believe he chose exile after a moment of clarity or guilt. Yarudvic neither confirms nor denies these tales, responding only with a faint smile and a change of subject.

## The Price of Admission

It is whispered that those who seek refuge in the Last Chance must reveal their darkest secrets to Yarudvic before they are allowed entry. While this is untrue, the belief persists, adding an air of mystery to his sanctuary.

## Protector of the Vulnerable

Many stories tell of Yarudvic protecting the Last Chance and its inhabitants with terrifying ferocity. One tale speaks of a gang of thieves who tried to rob the inn; they were never seen again, though a faint scent of brimstone lingered in the air for days.

## Yarudvic’s Philosophy

Yarudvic’s existence raises questions about the nature of morality and redemption. Despite his origins, he is committed to aiding those in need, even as he draws sustenance from their pain. He views negative emotions not as something to be eradicated, but as a natural and necessary part of existence.

## 1. The Cycle of Emotion

Yarudvic believes that pain and fear are inevitable, but they are also transient. He sees his role as helping others move through these emotions, rather than succumbing to them.

## 2. Free Will Above All

Unlike many Usurpers, Yarudvic does not demand loyalty or servitude. He values free will and sees himself as a facilitator rather than a master. Even his Chosen—rare mortals granted a fragment of his power—are free to use their abilities as they see fit, for good or ill.

## 3. Redemption as a Choice

Though Yarudvic’s past is shrouded in mystery, it is clear that he views redemption as a personal journey. He does not seek forgiveness, only to do what he can with the existence he has now.

## Cultural and Spiritual Impact

Yarudvic’s presence is both a source of fascination and unease in Aru’Mas.

- Admiration and Fear: To some, Yarudvic is a symbol of hope and healing, proof that even the darkest beings can choose a different path. To others, he is a reminder of the dangers of trusting a demon, no matter how benevolent they appear.
- A Quiet Ally: Yarudvic has earned the reluctant respect of the city’s rulers, including the Order of Harmony and the [[Sunspear Legion]], who recognize the good he does for the city. However, his nature as a Usurper ensures that his relationships remain cautious and distant.

## In Summary: The Paradox of Yarudvic

Yarudvic, the Usurper of the Last Chance, is a paradox: a demon who heals, a predator who nurtures, and a feared outsider who has become a vital part of the city’s fabric. His Claim stands as a sanctuary for the broken and the lost, a place where pain is acknowledged, processed, and ultimately eased.

To the people of Aru’Mas, Yarudvic is both a mystery and a marvel, a being who defies the boundaries of morality and reminds all who meet him that redemption, like the Dreaming, is a realm that exists just beyond reach—but always within sight.
