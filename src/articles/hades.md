# Hades

Hades — called the Gray Waste by most scholars — is a plane without joy, ambition, or emotional color of any kind. Souls there do not suffer in the dramatic manner of the Abyss or the Nine Hells; they simply stop wanting anything, over time, all positive feeling eroded by the plane's gray, lightless environment until nothing remains but a hollow that eventually becomes a larva. The horror of the Gray Waste is that it is quiet.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented relationship. The question of whether the Gray Waste's emotional erasure is related to the [[Faded Veil]]'s emotional drain — both planes removing feeling rather than inflicting pain — has been examined by scholars in Aru'Mas and produced no evidence of a connection. The mechanisms are believed to be distinct.
