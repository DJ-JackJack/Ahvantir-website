# Mechanus

Mechanus is an infinite clockwork plane of perfect order — its entire visible architecture composed of interlocking gears, cogs, and mechanisms of incomprehensible scale, each one turning in exact precision relative to every other, without pause and without deviation. It is not a place of law in any humanistic sense; it is a place of order in the mathematical sense, where every variable has a value and every value is accounted for.

**Ahvantir connection:** Mechanus is the residence and working domain of [[Lycanum Tyr Mechanus]], the god of Time, Fate, and True Order — and, as such, it is the only outer plane with a directly documented relationship to Ahvantir's theological establishment. Lycanum operates from Mechanus continuously, maintaining the machinery of fate from this plane rather than from [[The Ranjergon]] to which other deities are bound. It is also the only outer plane whose primary resident holds a permanent exemption from [[Deification in Ahvantir|the Divine Gate]].
