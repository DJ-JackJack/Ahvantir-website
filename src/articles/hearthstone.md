# Hearthstone

{% dmonly %}
Hearthstone has been officially resolved as **two distinct districts**: [[Upper Hearthstone]] and [[Lower Hearthstone]]. This article is retained as a historical overview and source record. For current lore, use the individual district articles.
{% enddmonly %}

Hearthstone is a lively residential district that embodies the industrious and hopeful spirit of Aru’Mas. Divided into two distinct sections—Upper Hearth and Lower Hearth—by the nearby Spillway, Hearthstone is home to a predominantly middle-class population. Its residents include skilled laborers, artisans, and their families, who have created a tightly knit community despite the challenges of being near the city’s more impoverished areas. Hearthstone serves as a metaphorical and literal bridge between the hardships of [[The Spillway|the Spillway]] and the opportunities of more prosperous districts like [[Temple Way]] and [[Spireview]].

## District Layout

## Upper Hearth

The Upper Hearth, located on slightly elevated ground, is the more established and affluent section of Hearthstone.

- Architecture and Streets:
- Homes in the Upper Hearth are neatly arranged and constructed from sturdy stone and polished wood, reflecting the pride and craftsmanship of the district’s residents. Narrow cobblestone streets wind through the area, lined with small parks, community gardens, and local gathering spaces.
- Community Life:
- Upper Hearth residents organize frequent events, including harvest festivals, artisan showcases, and charity drives to support their neighbors in the Lower Hearth and the Spillway. The community gardens are often tended collectively, providing fresh produce for local families and reinforcing the district’s strong sense of unity.
- Notable Areas:
- The Hearth Square: A bustling plaza where neighbors gather for celebrations, markets, and public meetings.
- Stonemason’s Guildhall: A modest but important building where skilled laborers coordinate work and apprenticeships, contributing to the district’s reputation for craftsmanship.

## Lower Hearth

The Lower Hearth, located south of the Spillway, has a grittier but no less vibrant character.

- Architecture and Streets:
- Homes in the Lower Hearth are smaller and more modest, often built with reclaimed materials or simpler designs. The streets are lively but less polished, with colorful murals and street art reflecting the creativity and resilience of the residents.
- Markets and Street Fairs:
- The Lower Hearth is known for its vibrant open-air markets and spontaneous street fairs. These gatherings showcase everything from handcrafted goods to exotic foods, with merchants, performers, and artisans adding to the district’s lively atmosphere.
- Challenges and Resilience:
- While the Lower Hearth struggles with the economic and social challenges of its proximity to the Spillway, its residents take immense pride in their ability to persevere. The strong sense of community ensures that neighbors look out for one another, sharing resources and support when needed.
- Notable Areas:
- The Spillgate Bazaar: A semi-permanent market near the border of the Spillway where vendors sell wares ranging from practical tools to handcrafted jewelry.
- The Lantern Walk: A path lit by hanging lanterns at night, providing a safe and inviting route through the district while symbolizing the residents’ determination to find light in the darkness.

## Life in Hearthstone

## The People

The residents of Hearthstone are defined by their industriousness, creativity, and strong community values.

- Skilled Laborers and Artisans: Many residents work as masons, carpenters, blacksmiths, and tailors, contributing to the city’s infrastructure and culture.
- Merchants and Vendors: The markets in both the Upper and Lower Hearth provide a livelihood for merchants who sell their goods to locals and travelers alike.
- Families: Hearthstone is a family-oriented district, with many multi-generational households that take pride in their contributions to the community.

## Daily Life

- Morning Hustle: The day begins with the sounds of hammering, the clinking of tools, and the chatter of merchants setting up their stalls.
- Evening Gatherings: At sunset, neighbors gather in public spaces to share meals, stories, and news, reinforcing the district’s strong sense of belonging.

## Subdistricts and Landmarks

## The Hearthforge

A small subdistrict within Upper Hearth, the Hearthforge is where many of the district’s artisans and craftspeople live and work. Blacksmiths, carpenters, and jewelers can be found here, their workshops bustling with activity. The Hearthforge Foundry, a communal forge, is a centerpiece of this area, providing tools and resources for local smiths.

## The Riverbend Commons

Located where the Lower Hearth meets the Spillway, the Riverbend Commons is a gathering place for residents of both sections. Its central feature is a modest but charming amphitheater used for performances, debates, and storytelling.

## Relationships with Other Districts

## Temple Way

Hearthstone’s proximity to Temple Way allows its residents to participate in the spiritual life of Aru’Mas. Many residents frequent the temples for blessings, healing, and guidance, and the district benefits from its connection to the [[Order of Harmony]].

## Spireview

Hearthstone’s skilled laborers and artisans often work on contracts for the [[Sunspear Legion]] and other military operations in Spireview, creating weapons, armor, and other supplies.

## The Spillway

Despite its challenges, the Spillway’s proximity fosters a sense of solidarity among Hearthstone residents. Many families in the Lower Hearth share ties with Spillway inhabitants, and community efforts often focus on improving conditions for their struggling neighbors.

## Cultural Identity

Hearthstone is a district of hope and determination, where residents take pride in their work and their community. It serves as a bridge between the hardships of the Spillway and the prosperity of Aru’Mas, showcasing the potential for resilience and cooperation to overcome adversity. Whether through the quiet strength of the Upper Hearth or the lively energy of the Lower Hearth, Hearthstone embodies the spirit of the people who call it home.
