# Carceri

Carceri — the Tarterian Depths — is a plane of imprisonment: six concentric spheres, each nested inside the next, each a different kind of prison from which escape is theoretically possible and in practice achieved by no one. The plane is ruled by betrayal, deception, and the specific despair of those who understand exactly how they arrived where they are.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented relationship. The question of whether the [[First Pact]]'s sealing of the [[The Primordem|Primordem]] beneath Ahvantir shares any cosmological logic with Carceri's imprisonment structure has been raised exactly once in surviving records — a margin note in the [[Ilderas Public Archive]] — and has not been seriously pursued.
