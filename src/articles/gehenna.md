# Gehenna

Gehenna is a plane of four volcanic mountains drifting in an endless void — their slopes too steep for easy footing, their volcanic activity a constant geological violence beneath every step, their atmosphere choking and hot. It is a plane of evil that is also entirely hostile to itself: even the yugoloths who call it home find nothing comfortable here, and the plane's defining quality is a mercilessness that extends to its own inhabitants without exception.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented relationship. No Ahvantir entity, cult, or significant figure has claimed Gehenna as a relevant domain in any current record.
