# The Sunspear Legion

The **Sunspear Legion** is the primary military force of Aru'Mas, forged in the founding era under the leadership of **Marduk Sunspear** himself. They serve as the city's standing army, protectors of the Crown's Approach district, and enforcers of martial law in times of crisis.

## History

During the founding of Aru'Mas, Marduk Sunspear rallied a coalition of warriors who would become the Legion's first cohort. Their victory over the original inhabitants of the region—later a source of great controversy—cemented the Legion's reputation as an unstoppable force.

In the Second Era, the Legion underwent significant reforms under Queen Vaylis, expanding from a personal guard into a professional army with ranks, standards, and a chain of command stretching into every district of the city.

## Structure

The Legion is divided into **Spires**—units of approximately 80 soldiers each. Each Spire is commanded by a **Spiremaster**, who answers directly to the **Grand Marshal** of the Legion.

- **First Spire** – Stationed in Crown's Approach, serves as the royal household guard
- **Second Spire** – Patrols Hearthstone and Stonegate
- **Third Spire** – Rapid response unit, barracks in Spireview

## Relationship with the City Militia

There is longstanding tension between the Sunspear Legion and the city militia. The Legion considers the militia a disorganized rabble; the militia resents the Legion's privilege and proximity to noble houses. In practice, the two forces rarely cooperate without friction.

## Symbols and Honours

The Legion's standard bears a golden sunburst on a crimson field—a deliberate echo of Marduk Sunspear's personal heraldry. Veterans of ten or more years' service are entitled to wear a small sunspear pin, a tradition dating to the founding era.
