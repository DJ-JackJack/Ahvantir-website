# Ysgard

Ysgard is a plane of glorious, endless struggle — floating landmasses drifting above rivers of fire and ice, populated by warriors who die each dusk and rise each dawn to fight again, not toward any objective but because the quality of the fight is its own reward. The plane venerates courage, physical excellence, and the refusal to stop, and those who dwell here permanently tend to find that the mortal world's stakes seem thin by comparison.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented relationship. No Ahvantir deity, faction, or significant figure has claimed Ysgard as a relevant domain or home plane in any current record.
