# Grand Speaker Artrice Vanderbane

- Title: Grand Speaker of the Merchants’ Guild of Aru’Mas
- Age: 48
- Affiliation: The Merchants’ Guild, the ruling council of Aru’Mas
- Base of Operations: The Guildhall, located in the bustling heart of the Grand Bazaar.

## Overview

Grand Speaker Artrice Vanderbane is the current head of the Merchants’ Guild of Aru’Mas and one of the three ruling council members of the city. A shrewd negotiator and skilled strategist, Artrice is known for her decisive leadership, keen understanding of multiversal trade dynamics, and her ability to navigate the complex politics of Aru’Mas. As Grand Speaker, she represents the economic interests of the city’s working class, traders, artisans, and entrepreneurs, making her one of the most powerful figures in the city.

Artrice has played a pivotal role in establishing Aru’Mas as a premier multiversal trading hub, pushing for aggressive economic growth and expanding the guild’s influence within and beyond the city’s boundaries. Her tenure as Grand Speaker has been marked by both significant successes and notable controversies, as her ambition to make Aru’Mas an unrivaled commercial center sometimes conflicts with the spiritual laws and Old Pacts that govern the archipelago.

## Background and Rise to Power

Artrice was born into a family of successful merchants who were among the early settlers of Aru’Mas. From a young age, she showed a natural talent for trade negotiation, business acumen, and a deep understanding of the city’s economic dynamics. By her mid-twenties, Artrice had already established herself as a textile magnate, specializing in importing rare and exotic fabrics from distant realms.

Her growing influence within the Artisans’ Council of the Merchants’ Guild positioned her as a key player in guild politics. Known for her bold decisions and innovative trade strategies, she rapidly ascended the ranks, becoming a member of the Guild Council and ultimately securing her election as Grand Speaker. Her success is attributed not only to her economic prowess but also to her ability to forge strategic alliances and maintain the guild’s unity amid competing interests.

## Goals and Ambitions

As Grand Speaker, Artrice’s primary goals are focused on the economic prosperity of Aru’Mas and the expansion of the city’s trading influence across multiversal markets.

## 1. Multiversal Trade Dominance

- Artrice is determined to establish Aru’Mas as the leading multiversal trade hub, pushing for broader trade agreements, reduced tariffs, and increased investments in infrastructure that support commerce. She has spearheaded efforts to secure exclusive trade deals with other realms, allowing for the import and export of exotic goods, rare magical artifacts, and unique resources.
- To achieve this goal, she has advocated for the expansion of the city’s port facilities and the construction of additional merchant districts to accommodate growing trade demands. Artrice believes that increasing the city’s commercial reach is essential not only for economic growth but also for solidifying its position as a center of power and influence.

## 2. Strengthening Guild Influence

- As a staunch advocate for the Merchants’ Guild, Artrice seeks to expand the guild’s influence within Aru’Mas’ political landscape. She is focused on ensuring that economic policies favor the guild’s interests, lobbying for laws that encourage entrepreneurship, reduce bureaucratic hurdles, and promote the guild’s control over key markets and trade routes.
- Artrice has also pushed for greater guild representation in diplomatic negotiations, arguing that the city’s economic interests should play a central role in Aru’Mas’ dealings with multiversal envoys and other governing bodies.

## 3. Balancing Economic Growth with Spiritual Compliance

- While Artrice is primarily driven by economic ambitions, she is aware of the need to respect the Old Pacts and the spiritual laws that govern Aru’Mas. She has initiated several initiatives to ensure that the guild’s expansion does not disrupt the city’s spiritual balance. This includes consulting with spiritual mediators from [[The Church of the Threefold Path|the Church of the Threefold Path]] and involving members of the [[Runeguard]] in trade negotiations that might impact spiritual boundaries.

## Personal Life and Family

Artrice’s personal life is marked by a blend of family loyalty and a fierce dedication to her role as Grand Speaker. While she keeps her personal affairs relatively private, her family connections are significant to her political influence.

## 1. Family Ties

- Artrice comes from the influential Vanderbane family, known for its control over the textile trade and various artisan sectors within the city. Her family members occupy prominent positions within the guild’s Artisans’ Council, giving her an additional layer of political support.
- She has two children, Elias and Selene, both of whom are being groomed to assume leadership roles within the guild. Elias has taken a more active role in the Artisans’ Council, showing promise as a skilled negotiator, while Selene is focusing on the port authority, managing logistics and maritime trade.

## 2. Personal Beliefs and Practices

- Artrice is known for her pragmatic approach to both life and leadership. She believes that prosperity and opportunity are the keys to the city’s success, often emphasizing the value of economic freedom in her speeches.
- Despite her business-like demeanor, she is a devoted mother, frequently spending time with her children and involving them in guild affairs to prepare them for future roles. Her devotion to her family is sometimes seen as a vulnerability by political rivals, who question whether her decisions are always made with the city’s best interests at heart.

## Strengths and Weaknesses

Artrice’s leadership is defined by a set of strengths and weaknesses that shape both her political strategies and her relationships within the city.

## Strengths

- Negotiation Skills: Artrice is a master negotiator, skilled at brokering deals that maximize profit while minimizing risk. Her ability to read people, identify leverage points, and strike favorable bargains has made her a formidable presence in both local and multiversal trade negotiations.
- Visionary Economic Strategies: As a leader, Artrice is known for her forward-thinking strategies, often implementing bold plans to expand the city’s commercial reach. Her focus on infrastructure development and innovation has led to several successful projects, such as the modernization of the Grand Bazaar and the construction of new merchant districts.
- Alliance Building: Artrice excels at building alliances with other influential figures, both within the city and beyond. She maintains strong relationships with the Artisans’ Council, key trade consulates, and even certain adventurers’ guilds, ensuring that the guild’s interests are well-represented in both political and economic matters.

## Weaknesses

- Ambition Over Spirituality: Artrice’s ambition often overshadows her understanding of Aru’Mas’ spiritual laws. While she tries to respect the Old Pacts, her focus on economic growth can sometimes lead to spiritual conflicts, particularly when trade expansion risks disrupting spiritual boundaries.
- Limited Patience for Bureaucracy: Artrice is known for her impatience with bureaucratic processes, often pushing for quick decisions to capitalize on trade opportunities. This approach can create friction with other council members, particularly [[High Priest Alabaster Sayn]], who prioritizes diplomacy and caution over rapid expansion.
- Family-Driven Decisions: Artrice’s devotion to her family occasionally influences her political decisions, leading to accusations of nepotism and favoritism within the guild. While her children are competent, her preference for keeping power within the Vanderbane family has led to some dissent among other guild members.

## Political Alliances and Conflicts

Artrice’s position as Grand Speaker puts her in constant interaction with other political and spiritual leaders, resulting in a complex web of alliances and rivalries.

## Alliances

- [[Heir Apparent|Crown Prince Kaelen]] Ilderas: Artrice shares a strong alliance with Crown Prince Kaelen, as both leaders are driven by ambitions of expansion and dominance. Artrice sees Kaelen’s military strategy as complementary to her economic vision, making them natural allies in pushing for the city’s broader growth.
- High Sage Lethyr ([[Order of Wisdom]]): Artrice maintains a cooperative relationship with High Sage Lethyr, recognizing the value of arcane research in developing magical goods and enhancing trade. She supports the Order of Wisdom’s initiatives, particularly when they align with the guild’s goals of magical innovation and resource acquisition.

## Conflicts

- High Priest Alabaster Sayn: Artrice’s relationship with Alabaster Sayn is marked by frequent tension, as the High Priest’s spiritual priorities often clash with her economic ambitions. While the two collaborate when necessary, Alabaster’s cautious approach to spiritual diplomacy often frustrates Artrice, who views it as an obstacle to progress.
- [[Scholar Princess|Princess Lira]] Ilderas: Artrice respects Princess Lira’s arcane expertise but finds her focus on spiritual harmony restrictive. The two have clashed over magical policies, especially when Lira’s research suggests potential dangers from trade-based rituals that could affect the Old Pacts.
