# Immanuel Faust

Immanuel Faust, an elven researcher and engineer from [[Aru'Mas]], is renowned for his groundbreaking contributions to the city’s infrastructure. Driven by a desire to alleviate the struggles of the less fortunate, particularly those in [[The Spillway|the Spillway]] district, Faust revolutionized urban development and remains a symbol of innovation and perseverance even after his transformation into an Automaton.

## Early Life and Career

Born and raised in the [[Upper Hearthstone]] district, Faust demonstrated exceptional intellect and a strong sense of empathy from a young age. His education at the prestigious [[Arrandak Academy]] honed his natural aptitude for arcane engineering. Motivated by the visible inequality in Aru'Mas, Faust dedicated himself to creating solutions that improved quality of life for all citizens.

Among his most notable accomplishments were:

- Tetherjump Network: A citywide system of teleportation pylons that significantly reduced travel times and connected Aru'Mas's districts, improving accessibility for all citizens.
- Arcane Stabilizers: Devices that secured Driftmoor Harbor’s floating airship platforms against strong winds, enabling safer operation and fostering economic growth.

## Tragic Death and Transformation

Faust’s ambition led him to pursue the creation of a new type of Automaton, designed to assist with dangerous or complex tasks. However, an overcharged arcane circuit caused a catastrophic explosion, taking his life. Unbeknownst to anyone at the time, Faust’s spirit became trapped in the unfinished Automaton prototype. Two decades later, his retired colleague, Freidrich Ardel, a gnomish engineer and close friend, decided to complete Faust’s final project. Upon activation, Ardel discovered that Faust’s consciousness had survived, embedded within the Automaton.

## Life as The Eternal Engineer

Now in his Automaton form, Immanuel Faust adapted to his new existence, leveraging his unique perspective to further his mission. His transformation, while physically limiting in some ways, allowed him to work tirelessly without the constraints of mortality. Key projects following his rebirth included:

- Automaton Assistants: Simplified constructs designed to assist workers in hazardous environments, particularly in impoverished districts like the Spillway.
- Energy-Conserving Technologies: Systems that provided stable, affordable arcane energy to underprivileged areas.
- Educational Initiatives: Collaborations with trade schools and Arrandak Academy to inspire young inventors and teach the ethics of innovation.

## Legacy

Faust’s transformation into "The Eternal Engineer" became a defining story in Aru’Mas. His Automaton body, crafted from [[Runic Steel]] and adorned with glowing arcane inscriptions, serves as a symbol of his enduring commitment to progress. His story is taught widely as a lesson in ambition, ethics, and resilience. Many view his rebirth as a miracle, while others regard it as a cautionary tale of the dangers of unchecked experimentation.
