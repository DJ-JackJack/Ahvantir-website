# Kristoff Valkyr

Kristoff Valkyr, known as "The King of Rags," is a shadowy and commanding figure in the criminal underworld of Aru’Mas, holding unparalleled sway over [[Ragmarket]]. His influence stretches across the city's hidden corridors and its most dangerous district, where his network of enforcers and intermediaries ensures his dominance. Despite his Oni heritage, Kristoff’s mastery of disguise allows him to pass as a tall, slender tiefling, a form he uses to navigate both the criminal and social spheres of Aru’Mas.

Kristoff originates from a world consumed by demonic hordes, where his power was eclipsed by far greater threats. His escape aboard the astral vessel *Goliath* marked the beginning of his transformation. In Aru’Mas, he leveraged his cunning and monstrous strength to carve out a place among the city’s criminals, rapidly rising to the top of Ragmarket’s volatile ecosystem.

## Criminal Operations

Kristoff’s empire spans a variety of illicit activities, and his title as "The King of Rags" is well earned due to his ability to orchestrate Ragmarket’s chaos to his advantage:

- Smuggling and Contraband: Kristoff oversees a sophisticated network that moves rare and illegal goods into and out of Aru’Mas. Whether it’s magical artifacts, restricted substances, or stolen treasures, his smugglers ensure that nothing enters the city unnoticed—and without a price.
- Information Control: Few secrets in Aru’Mas remain hidden from Kristoff’s web of informants. He collects information on everyone, from the city’s ruling council to rival gang leaders. This intelligence gives him unparalleled leverage, allowing him to manipulate events and eliminate threats before they materialize.
- Weapons Trade: Kristoff’s organization deals in high-quality arms, including enchanted and black-market weaponry. He supplies Ragmarket’s factions with the tools of their trade, ensuring their loyalty while maintaining a balance of power that prevents any single gang from threatening his rule.
- Assassinations and Enforcement: While Kristoff prefers subtlety and manipulation, he employs violence when necessary. His enforcers carry out targeted assassinations and public displays of force to remind his rivals of his power. Though Kristoff rarely engages in combat personally, his concealed Oni strength is a devastating surprise for those who underestimate him.
- Pacts and Deals: Drawing on his otherworldly origins, Kristoff facilitates forbidden bargains with spirits and other supernatural entities. His expertise in navigating these dangerous negotiations attracts clients desperate enough to pay his often steep and mysterious prices.

## The King of Rags

Ragmarket is Kristoff’s dominion, a chaotic district of shifting alliances and hidden dangers. While he holds no official title, his control is undeniable. Kristoff maintains a delicate balance among Ragmarket’s major gangs:

- Dragon’s Claws: He provides intelligence and occasional assistance to this honor-driven syndicate, keeping their ambitions in check while using them to enforce his will.
- [[Creeping Green]]: Though their eco-terrorist agenda runs counter to his interests, Kristoff trades in rare alchemical supplies they require, using their operations as a convenient distraction for his rivals.
- [[All Are One]]: Kristoff views the cult as a volatile and useful asset. He occasionally allows their drug, Haze, to flow into specific areas of Ragmarket to destabilize potential threats to his control.
- [[Iron Boots]]: He hires this paramilitary gang to enforce his rule when subtlety fails, ensuring Ragmarket never descends into unchecked chaos.

Kristoff’s position in Ragmarket is maintained through a combination of fear, respect, and calculated generosity. He ensures the district functions as a thriving black-market hub, enriching himself while keeping its factions too divided to challenge him.

## Methods and Reputation

Kristoff’s approach to power is defined by precision and control. His tiefling disguise, bolstered by his cunning and charisma, allows him to blend seamlessly into any setting. Those who deal with Kristoff describe him as calm, calculating, and utterly ruthless. His Oni strength, a closely guarded secret, has turned the tide of many confrontations in his favor, leaving survivors with tales of his monstrous capabilities.

Despite his ruthless nature, Kristoff values loyalty and competence above all. Those who serve him well are rewarded with wealth and protection, while betrayal or failure is met with swift and often public consequences.

## Legacy and Influence

As "The King of Rags," Kristoff Valkyr’s shadow looms large over Aru’Mas. His control of Ragmarket ensures his place as one of the city’s most powerful and dangerous figures. While some see him as a necessary evil, others view him as a threat to the city’s stability.
