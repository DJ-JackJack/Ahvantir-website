# Ilderas Dynasty

## The Royal Family of [[Aru'Mas]]

The Royal Family of Aru'Mas holds a unique position of influence within the city, representing the interests of the nobility while also maintaining a public image that resonates with the adventurous spirit of the people. Over the centuries, the royal family has cultivated a legacy of leadership, wisdom, and public service, blending aristocratic tradition with the bold and dynamic culture of the city. While they may not rule with absolute authority—being one of three key factions in the ruling council—their role remains significant in guiding the city’s future.

## Key Members of the Royal Family

## King [[Voren Ilderas]]

Title: King of Aru'Mas 
Age: 54 Role: As the sitting monarch, King Voren Ilderas represents the interests of the noble families of Aru'Mas on the ruling council. He is responsible for balancing the desires of the aristocracy with the needs of the wider population, as well as maintaining Aru'Mas’ reputation as a multiversal hub. Voren is a skilled diplomat and strategist, qualities that have been essential in managing the delicate relationships between the other council members, the [[Merchants Guild|Merchants' Guild]] and [[The Church of the Threefold Path]], as well as foreign powers who regularly visit Aru'Mas. Personality and Reputation: King Voren is known for his measured and pragmatic approach to leadership. He is seen as a stabilizing force in a city where multiversal politics, adventurers, and trade dynamics can create constant flux. His public image is that of a monarch who values order and tradition but is flexible enough to adapt to the rapidly changing needs of the city. However, he is sometimes criticized for his conservative stance on who can pass through Aru'Mas into the archipelago of Ahvantir. Many citizens see him as a fatherly figure—wise and steady—but others, especially among the more radical adventurers and traders, view him as somewhat cautious and unwilling to take risks. Public Perception: The nobility holds King Voren in high regard, viewing him as a protector of their interests and the upholder of the city's traditions. Among the general populace, however, opinions are more mixed. While he is respected for his wisdom and diplomacy, his cautious nature sometimes frustrates those seeking quicker action or broader access to the wealth and opportunities that Ahvantir represents.

## Queen [[Thara Ilderas]]

Title: Queen of Aru'Mas 
Age: 50 Role: Queen Thara Ilderas plays a more hands-on role in the governance of Aru'Mas than many expect of a royal consort. She is particularly involved in managing the royal estate and overseeing the city's diplomatic relationships, especially with foreign visitors. Her expertise in multiversal diplomacy is one of the reasons why Aru'Mas maintains such strong ties with far-flung realms and kingdoms. She is also the royal family's link to the [[Order of Harmony]], as she is known to be a devout follower of the Church of the Threefold Path. Personality and Reputation: Queen Thara is seen as a charismatic and compassionate leader who excels in diplomacy and public relations. She is beloved by the people for her frequent charity work and her involvement in improving the lives of the working class. Known for her intelligence and calm demeanor, Thara is adept at navigating complex political waters. Despite her noble status, she is a champion of Aru'Mas' multiversal identity, often advocating for greater cultural exchanges and fewer restrictions on who can enter the city. Public Perception: Queen Thara is wildly popular with the general public and is often considered the heart of the royal family. Her charm and generosity have made her beloved by people from all walks of life, particularly in the lower classes, who see her as a caring and approachable figure. Even adventurers and traders, who sometimes bristle at the monarchy’s conservative policies, appreciate her openness and willingness to listen.

## [[Heir Apparent|Crown Prince Kaelen]] Ilderas

Title: Heir Apparent to the Throne
Age: 29 Role: As the heir to the throne, Crown Prince Kaelen has been groomed to take on the responsibilities of leadership from a young age. While he is still learning the ropes of governance, he has already proven himself a capable leader, particularly in areas of defense and foreign relations. Kaelen has also shown an interest in working with the adventurers’ guilds, a sign that he may one day favor loosening some of the restrictions surrounding access to Ahvantir. Personality and Reputation: Kaelen is bold and ambitious, much like his mother, Queen Thara, but has inherited his father’s sense of duty and diplomacy. He has a reputation for being somewhat impatient with the slow-moving nature of traditional governance, and many see him as someone who will bring about significant changes when he ascends to the throne. Kaelen is also known for his prowess in combat and often trains with adventurers, occasionally joining them on minor quests, a rarity for someone of royal blood. This hands-on approach has endeared him to many. Public Perception: Among the youth and the more adventurous spirits of the city, Kaelen is highly admired. He represents a younger, more dynamic generation of leadership that many believe could steer Aru'Mas into a new golden age of adventure and expansion. The nobility, however, is cautious about his seemingly impulsive tendencies, fearing that he might disrupt the delicate balance that King Voren has worked hard to maintain. Nonetheless, Kaelen’s popularity continues to grow, especially among adventurers and guild members who see him as a potential ally.

## [[Scholar Princess|Princess Lira]] Ilderas

Title: Second Child of King Voren and Queen Thara
Age: 24 Role: Princess Lira, unlike her brother Kaelen, has little interest in politics or governance. Instead, she has become a prominent figure in the [[Order of Wisdom]], dedicating her life to scholarship, magic, and the pursuit of knowledge. Lira is particularly focused on understanding the mysteries of Ahvantir, and she has been involved in several expeditions, often as a magical consultant. She also oversees the royal archives and often acts as a liaison between the monarchy and the Order of Wisdom. Personality and Reputation: Princess Lira is intellectual and introverted, preferring the quiet solitude of study over the public life of courtly affairs. Despite her reserved nature, she is highly respected by the academic and magical communities of Aru'Mas for her intellect and contributions to magical research. Lira is fascinated by Ahvantir, particularly its unexplored depths and magical mysteries, and she is one of the monarchy’s strongest advocates for allowing more explorers and scholars to venture into the archipelago. Public Perception: Lira is seen as a quiet and enigmatic figure by the general public, largely because she avoids the spotlight. However, she is respected for her intellect and contributions to the city's magical advancement. Scholars and the magical elite, particularly within the Order of Wisdom, hold her in high regard, viewing her as a prodigy and a driving force behind the city’s intellectual pursuits.

## Prince [[Warrior Prince|Jorran Ilderas]]

Title: Youngest Child of the Royal Family
Age: 18 Role: Prince Jorran is the youngest member of the royal family and is still finding his place within the complex web of responsibilities and duties. Unlike his older siblings, Jorran has shown a strong interest in military affairs and physical combat. He trains frequently with the city guard and has aspirations to eventually lead Aru’Mas’ defense forces. His fiery, youthful spirit and dedication to protecting the city have earned him a reputation as a future defender of Aru'Mas. Personality and Reputation: Jorran is fiery, impulsive, and full of youthful energy. His ambition often leads him to make rash decisions, but he is determined to prove himself, especially in the eyes of his older siblings. His love for combat and defense has led him to form close ties with the [[Order of Valor]] and the city guard, where he has earned respect for his combat skills, despite his young age. Public Perception: The people view Jorran as a passionate and courageous young prince with the potential to become a powerful military leader in the future. However, his impulsive nature sometimes worries the more conservative factions of the city, who fear that his lack of experience could lead to reckless decisions. Nevertheless, many admire his bravery and are eager to see how his role within the city develops.

## The Royal Family’s Role and Influence in Aru'Mas

The royal family of Aru'Mas serves as a balancing force in the governance of the city, representing the interests of the noble class while also providing a stable, guiding hand in managing the city’s complex relationship with adventurers, traders, and multiversal visitors. Each member of the family plays a distinct role, from King Voren’s diplomacy to Queen Thara’s compassionate leadership, Kaelen’s adventurous spirit, Lira’s scholarly pursuits, and Jorran’s military ambitions. Together, they embody the city’s rich tapestry of tradition, exploration, and ambition. While the monarchy’s power is tempered by the ruling council, the royal family remains a deeply respected and influential force, shaping the city’s present and future while maintaining a strong connection with its adventurous and multiversal identity.
