### District Adjacencies and Proximity
- **Crowns Approach** sits at the top of the city, above Luminous Reach and Stonegate. It curves around the northern bay.
- **Luminous Reach** is a walled, prestigious enclave directly below Crowns Approach, bordered by Stonegate to the west and Aurora Song District to the south.
- **Stonegate** occupies the upper-centre-left. It borders Crowns Approach (north), Luminous Reach (east), Aurora Song District (south-east), Temple Way (south-west), and Hearthstone (south).
- **Temple Way** sits in the northwest of the city, a spiritual and pilgrimage district bordered by Upper Hearthstone (south), Aurora Song District (east), and Stonegate (north-east).
- **Aurora Song District** is the large central-right district, bordered by Stonegate (north-west), Temple Way (west), Luminous Reach (north), Spireview (south), and Driftmoor Haven (south-east).
- **Spireview** is the large lower-centre district — the administrative and military core. It is surrounded by Hearthstone (west), Aurora Song District (north), and Driftmoor Haven (east).
- **Hearthstone** (Upper and Lower) runs down the centre-left, bordered by Stonegate (north), Temple Way (north-west), Spireview (east), and The Spillway (south/west). Upper Hearthstone is the more northerly, walled half; [[Lower Hearthstone]] blends into the Spillway to the south.
- **Driftmoor Haven** occupies the lower-right and southern shore. It borders Aurora Song District (north), Spireview (west), and opens to the sea (east/south).
- **The Spillway** sits at the very bottom of the city, south-west. It is pressed between Hearthstone (north), Spireview (north-east), and Ragmarket (west, outside the walls).
- **Ragmarket** is outside the southern wall, west of The Spillway. It has no formal boundary with any district — it simply bleeds into the surrounding land.
- **The Gilded Bastion** is on its own island to the north-east, connected to the mainland (Crowns Approach) by a single heavily guarded bridge.

## District Descriptions

### Crowns Approach
The ceremonial and administrative heart of Aru'Mas. Every detail reflects the power, prestige, and grandeur of the city's monarchy. At its centre is the Gilded Bastion (royal palace), on its own private island accessible only via the heavily guarded Royal Archway. The Crowns Approach is a district of opulence, reserved for the royal family, high-ranking nobles, and the city's most celebrated figures. Its streets are immaculate, its buildings draped in gold and fine stone. Commoners are rarely seen here without official business.

### Luminous Reach
The most prestigious residential district in Aru'Mas, perched just below Crowns Approach. Synonymous with power, influence, and legacy — it houses the city's most prominent individuals, from noble families to decorated heroes. Elegant architecture, cobblestone streets, and lush greenery. Home to [[Arrandak Academy]] for the Gifted Few, the premier arcane institution in the city. Proximity to Crowns Approach confers enormous social status on residents.

### Temple Way
The spiritual heart of Aru'Mas, situated in the northwest of the city. What began as a modest cluster of temples devoted to the Pathite Pantheon has grown into a sprawling, multifaith district where faiths from across the multiverse converge. Dominated by the grand [[The Church of the Threefold Path|Church of the Threefold Path]], but also home to foreign temples, humble shrines, and wandering preachers. A vibrant community of pilgrims, spiritual leaders, artisans, and vendors. Bordered by Upper Hearthstone to the south, Aurora Song District to the east, and Stonegate to the north-east.

### Stonegate
A small but cherished district celebrated for its serenity, community, and proximity to the [[Spiritsway Passage]]. Primarily residential — modest, well-maintained homes, tightly knit community, strong sense of tradition and quiet dignity. A contrast to the bustle of larger districts. The Spiritsway Passage (the northern gate to the wilds) and [[The LaCroi Institute of Spiritology|the LaCroi Institute of Spiritology]] are located here. The [[Roost]] (Skywarden base) is also in the military sub-area of this district.

### Aurora Song District
A kaleidoscope of innovation, artistry, and cultural expression — the most vibrant and dynamic district in Aru'Mas. Every street is alive with colour, sound, and motion. Artisans, inventors, performers, and dreamers converge here. At its heart is the Oasis of Spirits, a lush park. Home to the [[Graveyard Rift]] (a gothic tavern), Miss Belliosa Blossom's Emporium of Exotic Entities, and the [[Horizon Watch Arena]] (in or adjacent to this district). The adventuring culture of the city has a strong presence here.

### Spireview
The militarised heart of Aru'Mas, defined by discipline, order, and purpose. Headquarters of the [[Sunspear Legion]]. Home to the [[Heartspire]] — the tallest structure in the city, serving as the administrative and spiritual centre. [[Galvantis Arms, Armor, and Automatons]] is located in its industrial area. Residents are a mix of soldiers, administrators, and support staff. Strict and organised — unlike Aurora Song or Driftmoor Haven. The Horizon Watch Arena, described as being "in the heart of the city," may straddle Aurora Song and Spireview.

### Hearthstone (Upper and Lower)
A lively residential district home to a predominantly middle-class population — skilled labourers, artisans, and their families. Historically one district, it was physically divided by the expansion of The Spillway. The [[Ilderas Public Archive]] is on the western edge (Upper Hearthstone area). [[Tarran Finn]]'s home is in Lower Hearthstone. **Upper Hearthstone** built high walls to seal itself off from the Spillway; **Lower Hearthstone** declined the walls and blended near-seamlessly into the Spillway's edges.

### Driftmoor Haven
The bustling gateway to Aru'Mas — its primary port and a major hub of commerce, culture, and multiversal exchange. Situated on the southern shore. The first part of the city most visitors encounter. Ships from across the seas and multiversal realms arrive at its docks. The [[Far-Farewell Market District]] is a major trading hub within it. Key locations include the [[Merchants Guild|Merchants' Guild]] Grand Hall, the [[Many Worlds Pot and Barrel]] tavern, Kragg's Menagerie of Marvels, and the Far Farewell Trade Co-op.

### The Spillway
The most impoverished of Aru'Mas's official districts — densely packed around the western gate, where formal city boundaries dissolve into a sprawl of makeshift homes and overburdened infrastructure. Narrow, winding alleyways. Proximity to Ragmarket casts a long shadow. Despite its hardships, the district teems with life and an enduring spirit of hope. The original immigrant overflow that split Hearthstone in two came from here. Lower Hearthstone's willingness to blend with the Spillway is its defining political characteristic.

### Ragmarket
A chaotic, ever-shifting settlement beyond the southern wall — outside the city's formal jurisdiction. Tents, makeshift shacks, and ramshackle pathways. Traditional laws hold little weight; power belongs to those who enforce their own rules. A haven for the criminal underworld, a refuge for outcasts, and a marketplace for illicit goods and services. Trust is a commodity here.

### Spiritsway Passage
**Coordinates:** lat=3170, lng=3112 | **District:** Stonegate (northern gate)

At the northernmost point of Aru'Mas — a towering gateway of stone and arcane magic, the sole entrance to the untamed wilds of the Ahvantir Archipelago. Fifty feet tall, formed from obsidian-veined granite covered in runic symbols representing the Old Pacts. Only the [[Order of the Keystone]] can open or close it. Adventurers must prove themselves at the Horizon Watch Arena before earning the right to pass. A sacred boundary between civilisation and wild magic.

### Arrandak Academy
**Coordinates:** lat=2566, lng=2924 | **District:** Luminous Reach

The most prestigious institution of higher learning in Aru'Mas, specialising in magical studies, spirit research, and scholarly pursuits. ### The LaCroi Institute of Spiritology
**Coordinates:** lat=2523, lng=2419 | **District:** Stonegate

Named after the Giff scientist [[Erand Frond Marsad Pinkerton LaCroi]]. Dedicated to the scientific study of spirits, their behaviours, and their interactions with the natural world. Unlike Arrandak Academy, it emphasises empirical research over spellcraft. One of the few institutions in Aru'Mas approaching spirits from a naturalist rather than arcane or religious perspective. Located in Stonegate — near the Spiritsway Passage, which makes it convenient for field study of spirits beyond the city walls.

### Horizon Watch Arena
**Coordinates:** lat=2320, lng=3109 | **District:** Luminous Reach / Aurora Song border

A renowned proving ground and entertainment venue managed by the Adventurers' Guild. ### Ilderas Public Archive
**Coordinates:** lat=1344, lng=1148 | **District:** Hearthstone (Upper, western edge)

On the western edge of Aru'Mas, established centuries ago by the Ilderas royal family. Both a public library and a revered institution preserving history, culture, and magical knowledge — books, manuscripts, scrolls, and artifacts. The main collection is freely accessible to citizens and travellers alike. Restricted sections require special permission to enter. A rare institution in that it is open to all regardless of class or origin.

### Galvantis Arms, Armor, and Automatons
**Coordinates:** lat=1128, lng=1496 | **District:** Spireview (industrial heart)

A technological and cultural marvel — a towering foundry of blackened steel and glowing forges, with a constant hum of machinery. Warforged staff serve as both creators and caretakers, blending mechanical precision with artisanal craftsmanship.
