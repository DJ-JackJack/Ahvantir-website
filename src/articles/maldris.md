# Maldris

Maldris, the Blade of Betrayal, is a cunning and manipulative deity within the ranks of the [[Eight Shadows]], focused on fostering betrayal, disloyalty, and treachery among the people of Aru’Mas. His goal is to undermine the bonds of trust that [[The Church of the Threefold Path|the Church of the Threefold Path]] works so diligently to build, especially the loyalty and unity promoted by the [[Order of Valor]]. Maldris takes a perverse pleasure in turning allies against each other, sowing seeds of discord in the closest of relationships, from friends and family to military units and governmental factions.

Maldris's followers see him as the ultimate opportunist, one who believes in self-preservation and manipulation over loyalty and honesty. His cult is skilled at hiding in plain sight, exploiting moments of vulnerability to plant subtle doubts, foster jealousy, and turn trust into resentment.

## Symbolism and Appearance

Maldris is typically depicted as a figure holding a dagger hidden behind his back, symbolizing both his capacity for deception and his belief in betrayal as an effective tool. His symbol, a cracked mirror reflecting two faces—one forward-facing, the other turned away—is often used in subtle carvings, sigils, or small amulets worn by his followers. The cracked mirror represents the dual nature of trust and betrayal, the divide that Maldris seeks to widen in every relationship he touches.

## Cults and Followers

Maldris’s influence can be found within two primary cults that operate discreetly in Aru’Mas and its outlying regions. Known as the Veil of Knives and the Silent Fracture, these groups employ manipulation, deceit, and subtle violence to achieve Maldris's ends.

## The Veil of Knives

The Veil of Knives is a clandestine organization within Aru’Mas, composed of spies, manipulators, and skilled assassins. They are not a large cult, but their members have infiltrated critical areas of the city’s infrastructure, including the merchant guilds, the city guard, and some lower-ranking positions within the Church itself. The Veil of Knives excels in using espionage and intrigue to sow distrust, particularly among powerful figures and close-knit groups.

- Methods of Influence: The Veil of Knives plants agents who appear loyal but secretly work to foster division and suspicion. For example, they may spread rumors of corruption or create false documents that implicate leaders in scandalous or treacherous acts.
- Sabotaging Relationships: They often target leaders, high-ranking officials, and prominent families. Their goal is to isolate individuals by turning friends and allies against each other, sometimes even using blackmail to force people into betrayal.
- Hidden Shrines: The Veil of Knives maintains small, hidden shrines to Maldris throughout the city, typically in shadowed, enclosed places where betrayal thrives. These shrines are marked with tiny mirrors and daggers set in wall crevices or alleyways, allowing followers to pay homage discreetly. Prayers to Maldris often involve vows of self-preservation at any cost, invoking his power to hide their intentions and sharpen their instincts.

## The Silent Fracture

The Silent Fracture operates primarily in the Fend and focuses on eroding trust within communities and family units. Unlike the Veil of Knives, who target the powerful and influential, the Silent Fracture preys on the common folk, spreading mistrust between friends, neighbors, and family members. They work subtly, blending into everyday life and using quiet whispers and calculated acts to create resentment and suspicion.

- Encouraging Paranoia in Communities: The Silent Fracture thrives in small, tight-knit communities where they can carefully plant suspicions. They foster paranoia, making community members question the motives of their neighbors or fear betrayal from their closest companions.
- Exploiting Pacts with Spirits: In the Fend, where pacts with spirits are essential to daily life, the Silent Fracture often targets these agreements. They may plant the idea that certain individuals are exploiting these pacts for personal gain, leading others to doubt the trustworthiness of both the pacts and the people upholding them.
- Shrines in Abandoned Buildings: Members of the Silent Fracture establish shrines in abandoned or hidden parts of the Fend, often places that families or communities have abandoned after disputes. These shrines are marked with cracked symbols and remnants of broken items, symbolizing fractured trust. Followers believe that Maldris’s presence grows stronger in places marked by betrayal.

## Goals and Methods

Maldris's ultimate goal is to corrode the bonds of trust and loyalty that bind people together, creating a society where betrayal and self-interest dominate over mutual support and unity. His followers work through manipulation, subtle threats, and deceitful promises, turning small misunderstandings into deep divides.

## Strategies to Undermine the Church

- Sowing Discord in the Order of Valor: The Order of Valor, with its emphasis on loyalty and honor, is one of Maldris’s primary targets. The Veil of Knives frequently attempts to infiltrate the Order with members who are willing to spread distrust among the ranks. By undermining the Order’s cohesion, they weaken the protection that the Order of Valor provides to the people of Aru’Mas.
- Corrupting the Council of Aru’Mas: Members of the Veil of Knives work to destabilize the city’s governing bodies by planting false evidence of corruption or betrayal among council members. This has the dual effect of weakening faith in the government and leading officials to distrust one another, stalling the decision-making process and allowing the cult to operate more freely.
- Eroding Community Trust in the Fend: The Silent Fracture seeks to turn Fendfolk against one another by playing on suspicions about pacts and the uneven distribution of spiritual protection. By fostering distrust, they aim to weaken the Fendfolk’s reliance on each other, leaving them vulnerable and divided.
- Promoting False Prophets: Both the Veil of Knives and the Silent Fracture occasionally elevate members as “visionaries” or “advisors” who claim to offer insights but instead subtly twist the teachings of the Pathite Pantheon to justify self-serving actions. These false prophets speak of “hidden truths” about [[Vesira]], [[Orenn]], or [[Alor]], steering followers toward divisive beliefs.

## Maldris’s Temptations and Gifts

Maldris tempts individuals with promises of power over others, protection from betrayal, and the ability to achieve their ambitions by any means necessary. Those who seek his favor may receive the following abilities:

- Veil of Deception: Maldris grants his followers the ability to project a false aura of trustworthiness, making it easier for them to deceive and manipulate those around them. This ability helps them avoid detection and allows them to infiltrate groups that would otherwise be resistant to their influence.
- Touch of Treachery: Maldris’s followers gain a subtle aura that fosters distrust and resentment in those nearby. This passive effect causes allies and close friends to question each other, amplifying minor grievances into festering doubts.
- Dagger’s Edge: Particularly devoted followers are granted the power to summon a spectral dagger, a weapon of betrayal, which grows stronger if used to harm someone who once trusted the wielder. This spectral dagger cannot be easily dispelled, a dark gift from Maldris symbolizing his pleasure in acts of betrayal.

## Church Countermeasures Against Maldris

The Order of Valor and [[Order of Harmony]] are particularly active in countering Maldris’s influence, as his methods primarily attack trust, loyalty, and community ties. Each Order has developed specific rituals and practices to prevent his influence from taking root.

- The Bonding Oath Ceremony: The Order of Valor conducts a bonding ceremony where members swear loyalty to one another and to the people of Aru’Mas. By reaffirming these vows of trust, they seek to create a unified front that resists betrayal. This ritual is often attended by city guards, soldiers, and those involved in public service.
- Trust Reinforcement Circles: The Order of Harmony holds gatherings where community members come together to share grievances in a supportive, non-judgmental environment. These circles encourage individuals to speak openly, reducing the effectiveness of Maldris’s followers who rely on isolation and secrecy to plant seeds of betrayal.
- Rite of Truth’s Light: This ritual, conducted by the [[Order of Wisdom]], focuses on exposing lies and promoting transparency. By encouraging individuals to be open about their thoughts and actions, the Order of Wisdom hopes to counter the duplicity of Maldris’s followers and prevent misunderstandings from escalating into deeper conflicts.

## Summary

Maldris, the Blade of Betrayal, is a malevolent deity who seeks to turn the people of Aru’Mas against each other, eroding the bonds of trust that hold society together. Through his cults, the Veil of Knives and Silent Fracture, he subtly spreads discord and suspicion, targeting both the powerful within the city and the close-knit communities of the Fend. Maldris’s followers, with their dark gifts and manipulative influence, are masters of deception, working tirelessly to achieve his goal of a fractured and mistrustful society.

However, through the efforts of the Church—particularly the Orders of Valor, Harmony, and Wisdom—the people of Aru’Mas are provided with support and guidance to resist his influence. Despite the constant threat posed by Maldris and his followers, the bonds within Aru’Mas remain strong, fortified by rituals, transparency, and mutual understanding.
