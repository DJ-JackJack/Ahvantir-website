# Faded Veil

## The Shadowed Reflection of Ahvanti

"A land of shifting shadows and whispering doom, where even memories are not safe." The Faded Veil, often simply called the Fade, is a sinister plane that mirrors the Material Plane, warping its landscapes into grim, twisted reflections of reality. It is a realm suffused with negative energy, the wellspring of necrotic magic and undeath. The plane is ruled by a cabal of eldritch entities known as the Iron Bound Kings, each governing vast dominions of despair and corruption.

## Nature of the Fade

The Faded Veil is a place of eternal twilight, where the sky remains an overcast, featureless expanse of gray. There is no sun, no stars—only an ambient gloom that suffuses the world. Structures from the Material Plane have dark counterparts in the Fade, appearing as crumbling ruins, endless graveyards, or monolithic fortresses of shadow. The very air carries an unnatural chill, sapping warmth and dulling emotions. The ground shifts and reshapes itself without warning, causing paths to change or disappear. The shadows, however, are the most unsettling—they watch, they move, and they whisper.

## Psychic Corruption and Emotional Devouring

One of the greatest dangers of the Fade is its relentless assault on the mind and soul. Unlike most planes where threats are physical or magical, the Fade preys upon a traveler's very psyche. • Whispering Shadows – The darkness around you murmurs constantly, dredging up your worst fears and regrets. These voices are insidious, making even the most confident individual doubt themselves. • Fractured Bonds – Companions traveling together find their unity tested. The Fade sows paranoia, making travelers suspicious of one another, causing miscommunications and mistrust to fester. • Emotional Drain – Over time, the plane leeches away positive emotions, replacing them with hollowness. Even remembering joyful moments becomes an uphill struggle. • Memory Consumption – The most insidious danger of the Fade is its ability to devour memories. Travelers who attempt to recall comforting moments find those memories fading—stolen by the plane itself. The longer one remains, the greater the risk of forgetting their identity entirely.

## Denizens of the Fade

The Faded Veil is not empty; it is home to an array of haunting entities, each shaped by the plane’s malevolent will. • The Hollowed – Wandering spirits that have lost all identity, their faces blurred and featureless. They lash out at travelers, hoping to steal fragments of their consciousness to fill the void within themselves. • The Grinning Ones – Humanoid shadows wearing masks of bleached bone, eternally grinning. They offer help and make bargains, but their deals always come with unforeseen consequences. • The Iron Bound Kings – The tyrannical rulers of the Fade, each an ancient and powerful entity bound in chains of cursed iron. They rule from massive fortresses, each reflecting their unique brand of suffering and despair. • Dread Echoes – Residual specters of past tragedies, endlessly reliving their torment. Unwary travelers may find themselves drawn into these echoes, experiencing horrors that are not their own.

## Surviving the Fade

Few enter the Faded Veil willingly, but those who do must prepare for more than mere monsters. • Bribing or Bargaining – Some denizens, particularly the Grinning Ones, can be bribed or tricked into offering temporary sanctuary. However, their favors often come with unseen costs. • Shadow Tokens – Rare iron coins, known as Shadow Tokens, provide protection from the plane’s influences. Each token grants safe passage through the gloom once, before crumbling into dust. • Strong Wills and Rituals – The best defense against the Fade’s whispers is mental fortitude. Many travelers repeat mantras, cling to meaningful relics, or perform rituals of protection to maintain their sense of self. • Light and Fire – While normal fire casts little illumination in the Fade, consecrated flames—such as those blessed by divine magic—can push back the encroaching darkness for a time.

## Exiting the Fade

Leaving the Faded Veil is not as simple as finding a portal. Those who succumb to its effects may forget that they ever wanted to leave, resigning themselves to a fate of wandering endlessly in the gloom. Even those who escape often bring something back with them—the echo of a whisper, the lingering chill of the shadows, or an unsettling affinity for necrotic magic. Some become haunted by the plane, suffering visions or nightmares that slowly pull them back. The Call of the Fade—A terrible fate awaits those who have stayed too long: the Fade remembers them. Even after they leave, it calls to them in dreams, whispering secrets and luring them back. Few can resist the second call. "In the Fade, all roads lead to ruin. Walk carefully, and never listen too closely to the whispers." ..
