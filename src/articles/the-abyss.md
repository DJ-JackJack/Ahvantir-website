# The Abyss

The Abyss is the largest and most contested of the evil outer planes — infinite descending layers, each a self-contained domain of chaotic malice ruled by a demon lord, each worse than the one above it in ways that don't follow a predictable scale. It does not organize in any stable sense; its demons exist to corrupt and destroy, and its politics are those of an ongoing war of everyone against everyone that has never come close to resolution.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented incursion of Abyssal origin has occurred on Ahvantir in accessible scholarship. Given the convergent nature of Aru'Mas's population — which draws travelers, refugees, and curiosities from across the multiverse — demon-touched individuals or objects from other worlds have plausibly passed through the city's history, but nothing has been confirmed.
