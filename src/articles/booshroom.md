# Booshroom

(Phantasmagoria Fungica)

The Booshroom is a rare and highly sought-after fungus native to the depths of Ahvantir’s ancient forests and the hidden caverns beneath its surface. This spectral mushroom is instantly recognizable by its eerie bioluminescent glow and distinctive cap, which bears two dark spots resembling hollowed-out eyes. Revered for its unique connection to the spirit world, the Booshroom is both a tool of curiosity for scholars of the arcane and a dangerous temptation for thrill-seekers willing to risk the unknown.

## Physical Characteristics

The Booshroom has a delicate, translucent stalk that shimmers faintly under direct moonlight. Its cap, a ghostly pale white, emits a soft, greenish-blue glow, making it visible even in pitch darkness. The two black "eye spots" on the cap give it an uncanny resemblance to a spectral face, contributing to its eerie reputation. The fungus is spongy yet firm to the touch and exudes a faint, ozone-like scent that intensifies when disturbed.

## Ecology & Habitat

The Booshroom thrives in places where the boundary between life and death is thinnest. It is most commonly found in:

- Ancient burial sites
- Long-abandoned ruins
- Deep underground caverns

It flourishes in environments rich in decaying organic matter, feeding off the residual energies of the departed. Some scholars theorize that Booshrooms grow where past spirit activity has been particularly strong, suggesting that their spores might be carried on the ethereal winds that whisper through Ahvantir’s haunted places. The fungus does not appear in cultivated soil, resisting any attempts at domestication, and seems to require an intrinsic connection to latent spiritual energies to germinate.

## Magical Properties & Effects

When consumed, the Booshroom grants the user a temporary state of etherealness, allowing them to interact with ghosts and move through solid objects as though they were a spirit themselves. This effect lasts for up to 10 minutes, during which the user can experience the world as a wandering soul, slipping past barriers and communicating with lingering spirits.

[spoiler=The Price of the Booshroom]
However, this experience comes at a steep cost. 1d8 hours after ingestion, the consumer falls into a deep unconscious state, as if teetering on the edge of life and death. They immediately begin making death-saving throws, even if they are otherwise unharmed. During this state, the user's spirit is believed to momentarily slip into the afterlife, and with a successful roll, they may encounter a deity or planar being who imparts guidance or a cryptic message that could alter their fate.

The Booshroom’s effects are unpredictable, and many who consume it do not return from the experience. Though stabilizing the affected individual is theoretically possible, some healers report that those who ingest the fungus have already begun their journey into the beyond, and only divine intervention or extraordinary measures can truly save them.
[/spoiler]

## Cultural Significance

Throughout Ahvantir, the Booshroom holds a place of reverence, superstition, and controversy.

- Shamans, spirit binders, and necromancers see it as a powerful tool for communing with the dead. Some sects of [[The Church of the Threefold Path|the Church of the Threefold Path]], particularly those devoted to [[Orenn]], believe consuming the fungus allows individuals to glimpse the weave of fate itself.
- The Fendfolk view the Booshroom as a sacred gift, meant only for those who wish to seek the wisdom of their ancestors. Its collection is often restricted to spiritual elders, who perform elaborate rites before harvesting one.
- Criminals and thrill-seekers sometimes take the risk of consuming a Booshroom as a reckless act of bravado, hoping to return with knowledge or visions that could give them an edge.
- Many scholars of Aru’Mas seek the fungus for study, fascinated by its connection to the afterlife and its apparent ability to pierce the veil between planes. However, the dangers associated with its use make it highly controversial in academic circles.

## Harvesting & Availability

Due to its spectral nature, harvesting a Booshroom is no simple task. The fungus often vanishes at the slightest disturbance, fading into nothingness if not approached with care. Skilled foragers and spirit binders use specialized rituals or enchanted tools to stabilize the mushrooms long enough to collect them.

Because of its extreme rarity and potent effects, a single Booshroom commands an exorbitant price on the black market. Some alchemists have attempted to extract its essence to create a safer alternative, but all known attempts have either failed or resulted in unstable, lethal concoctions.

## Warnings & Risks

Consumption of a Booshroom is never without risk. The delayed, unavoidable descent into unconsciousness makes it a dangerous gamble, and many who partake in its effects do not return. Some who have barely survived report that their spirits lingered too long in the afterlife, leaving them forever haunted by whispers and visions of what lies beyond.

Despite its dangers, the Booshroom remains one of Ahvantir’s most enigmatic and sought-after natural wonders, its spectral glow a beacon for the curious and the foolhardy alike. ..
