# Education System of [[Aru'Mas]]

The city of Aru’Mas boasts a structured and accessible education system that provides free foundational learning for children, literacy programs for adults, and specialized training in various trades and arcane disciplines. The system is designed to ensure that all citizens possess essential knowledge while allowing for further specialization in skilled professions, magic, and spiritual studies. The pinnacle of this educational structure is the prestigious [[Arrandak Academy]] for the Gifted Few, an institution dedicated to advanced magical, scholarly, and spiritual studies.

## 1. Public Education for Children

The city mandates free public education for all children between the ages of 5 and 14, ensuring that literacy and numeracy are universally accessible. The curriculum focuses on foundational knowledge while also preparing students for potential future paths in trades, academia, or magical studies. Schools are available in every district, with funding provided by the city and religious donations, primarily from the [[Order of Wisdom]].

## Core Curriculum:

- Literacy and Numeracy: Reading, writing, and basic arithmetic.
- History and Civics: The history of Ahvantir, the founding of Aru’Mas, and an introduction to the Old Pacts.
- Natural Sciences: Basic education in botany, alchemy, and physics.
- Ethics and Law: Morality, civic duties, and legal rights.
- Spirit-Pact Awareness: The rules and significance of spirit interactions, recognizing common spirits, and understanding the consequences of violating pacts.
- Basic Magical Awareness: While full magic training is reserved for specialized institutions, children with latent magical abilities are identified through standardized assessments conducted by the Order of Wisdom and provided with preliminary control exercises.

Students who excel in particular subjects are encouraged to pursue further education through apprenticeships, trade schools, or specialized academies.

## 2. Adult Education & Literacy Programs

Aru’Mas offers a variety of programs to ensure that adults who missed early schooling or immigrants unfamiliar with local customs can integrate effectively into society. These classes are primarily held in public schools and temples, often taught by volunteers or clergy members.

## Key Offerings:

- Basic Literacy and Numeracy: Essential for personal and business dealings.
- Magical Literacy for Commoners: Training in recognizing runes, identifying common magical artifacts, and understanding magical laws.
- Spirit-Pact Education for Travelers and Merchants: Courses on interacting safely with spirits, particularly those governing the seas and trade routes.

Specialized evening courses are also available for adults seeking professional advancement in areas such as commerce, advanced mathematics, and spirit negotiations.

## 3. Specialized Trade Schools

For those who complete basic education, Aru’Mas offers an extensive network of trade schools that provide practical training for a nominal fee. Many of these institutions are affiliated with professional guilds, ensuring a seamless transition from education to employment.

## Fields of Study:

- Rune-Crafting and Enchantment: Training in inscribing runes onto tools, weapons, and armor, as well as basic enchantment techniques.
- Alchemy and Potion Brewing: Herbalism, alchemical transmutation, and practical applications in medicine and commerce.
- Combat Training and Warfare Studies: Military schools affiliated with the [[Order of Valor]] train warriors in combat tactics, martial disciplines, and battle magic.
- Spirit-Infused Professions: Specialized training for trades that require spiritual knowledge, such as shipbuilding with respect to sea spirits and architectural rune-work for protective enchantments.
- Artisan Guild Training: Textile weaving, glassblowing, smithing, and other crafts enhanced with magical elements.

Many trade schools operate apprenticeship programs that place students in established businesses for hands-on experience.

## 4. Arrandak Academy for the Gifted Few

The pinnacle of education in Aru’Mas, Arrandak Academy for the Gifted Few, is a prestigious university dedicated to magical, scholarly, and spiritual studies. Entry into the academy is highly competitive, requiring exceptional talent, noble sponsorship, or outstanding academic achievement.

## Areas of Study:

- Advanced Magical Studies: High-level spellcasting, leyline theory, and arcane rituals.
- Spirit Studies and Pact-Making: Research into the nature of spirits, negotiation techniques, and the ethics of spirit-binding.
- Spiritual Diplomacy and Conflict Resolution: Training in mediating disputes between spirits and mortals, as well as upholding the city's spiritual laws.
- Runic Engineering: The development of magical artifacts and integration of rune-work into everyday life.
- Comparative Theology and Cultural Studies: Understanding religious systems and their influence on magic and spirit interactions.
- Historical and Arcane Research: Investigations into the past to uncover lost magical knowledge and spiritual artifacts.

## Notable Features:

- A vast restricted library containing ancient texts and rare tomes on magic and spirits.
- Supervised practical spirit interaction programs for students specializing in pact-making and spiritual negotiations.
- Research laboratories dedicated to the study of the intersection between arcane forces and spirit energies.
- An esteemed faculty composed of leading scholars, high-ranking members of the Order of Wisdom, and experienced spirit-binders.

Arrandak Academy serves as a center for innovation and scholarship, drawing some of the brightest minds from across Ahvantir and beyond.

## 5. Additional Educational Institutions & Mentorship Programs

Beyond formal schooling, various mentorship and independent study programs exist within Aru’Mas.

## Independent Spirit Scholars:

Wandering sages and spiritualists offer personal mentorship in non-traditional methods of spirit interaction, often taking on a few dedicated students who show promise.

## The Order of Wisdom’s Influence:

Many magical instructors and historians come from the Order of Wisdom, ensuring that ethical considerations remain central to education.

## Military and Combat Training:

The Order of Valor operates specialized combat academies that integrate martial prowess with arcane techniques, training elite warriors and defenders of Aru’Mas.

## Conclusion

The education system of Aru’Mas reflects the city's deep respect for knowledge, magic, and spiritual harmony. From free foundational education to elite magical training, the city ensures that its citizens are well-prepared for the complexities of life in Ahvantir. The integration of spirit studies and pact education into early schooling underscores the city's reliance on the Old Pacts, while trade schools and advanced institutions like Arrandak Academy provide pathways for individual growth and societal advancement. As Aru’Mas continues to thrive, its educational institutions remain a cornerstone of its prosperity and stability.
