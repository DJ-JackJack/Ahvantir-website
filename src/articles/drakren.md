# Drakren

Drakren, the Architect of Fear, is a dark and insidious deity within the [[Eight Shadows]], representing fear, paranoia, and isolation. Drakren thrives on the distrust and anxiety that arises when people feel unsafe, amplifying the shadows in their minds until they begin to suspect everyone around them. His influence is particularly dangerous to the unity and cohesion of Aru’Mas, as he seeks to shatter trust within communities, families, and even between the citizens and [[The Church of the Threefold Path|the Church of the Threefold Path]]. Drakren opposes the values of courage, unity, and resilience championed by the [[Order of Valor]].

Drakren’s followers believe that fear is the most powerful force, capable of controlling and manipulating people to achieve their ends. They view fear as both a weapon and a shield, and they work to spread paranoia and mistrust throughout Aru’Mas, gradually eroding the foundations of community and solidarity.

## Symbolism and Appearance

Drakren is often depicted as a tall, cloaked figure with hollow, piercing eyes that seem to see into the darkest parts of the soul. His symbol, a pair of watchful eyes surrounded by shadows, represents his ability to see through masks and expose hidden fears. Followers often carry tokens with these eyes or wear dark clothing to signify their devotion. The cult of Drakren favors shadows and darkness, where they believe his influence is strongest, and use subtle symbols like the Whispering Mask—a mask painted with the outline of wide, staring eyes.

## Cults and Followers

Drakren’s followers, known as The Silent Eyes, are a clandestine cult that operates in secrecy and relies on subtlety and manipulation. They do not rely on overt displays of power; rather, they use psychological tactics to instill fear, encourage distrust, and weaken social bonds. The Silent Eyes are found among a variety of professions, including bureaucrats, informants, and even some low-ranking members of the city’s guard.

## The Silent Eyes

The Silent Eyes are a cult dedicated to Drakren’s ideals of fear and isolation. They operate through rumor, subtle threats, and psychological manipulation, preferring to let their influence spread quietly and infectiously. Members are trained in the art of misdirection, using fear as a tool to control situations without drawing attention to themselves.

- Methods of Influence: The Silent Eyes use whispered rumors, subtle sabotage, and intimidation to achieve their goals. They spread tales of danger lurking in the city, plant seeds of mistrust between allies, and create a general atmosphere of fear. Members often leave cryptic messages or ominous symbols to unsettle their targets.
- Creating a Culture of Paranoia: The Silent Eyes work to make people question the intentions of those around them. By suggesting that even friends or family members could be harboring ill intent, they encourage people to isolate themselves, creating fractured communities and a society rife with suspicion.
- Sabotaging Trust in the Church and the Order of Valor: The cult often targets the Order of Valor, spreading rumors of corruption within its ranks or suggesting that certain members cannot be trusted. They also work to erode the Church’s influence by casting doubt on its ability to protect the city, implying that the Church’s power is not enough to keep the citizens safe.
- Hidden Shrines in Dark, Secluded Areas: Shrines to Drakren are concealed in abandoned or shadowy areas of the city, such as alleys, hidden rooms, or basements. These shrines are adorned with symbols of eyes and unsettling imagery. Followers gather here in small groups, meditating on their fears and leaving tokens of secrecy, such as personal items or scraps of dark fabric, as offerings.

## Goals and Methods

Drakren’s ultimate goal is to shatter trust and solidarity within Aru’Mas, leaving its citizens fearful, isolated, and vulnerable. By encouraging people to give in to their suspicions and fears, he seeks to create a fractured society where no one feels safe, weakening the city’s defenses against external and internal threats alike.

## Strategies to Undermine the Church

- Infiltrating the Order of Valor and Spreading Rumors: The Order of Valor, with its emphasis on courage and loyalty, is one of Drakren’s main targets. The Silent Eyes often infiltrate the Order to sow seeds of distrust among its members, making them suspicious of each other’s motives and creating internal divisions.
- Casting Doubt on the Church’s Protection: The Silent Eyes spread rumors that the Church of the Threefold Path is unable to protect the city from dark forces, suggesting that even the deities of the Pathite Pantheon may not be powerful enough to shield the people from harm. This tactic is intended to make citizens feel vulnerable and lose faith in the Church’s guardianship.
- Staging Apparent Acts of Malevolence: The Silent Eyes orchestrate incidents that appear to be malicious attacks, leaving clues that imply someone within the community is responsible. This creates paranoia, as people begin to wonder if their friends, neighbors, or even family members are secretly working against them.
- Encouraging Superstitions and Fear of the Unknown: The Silent Eyes foster superstition and irrational fear, particularly among those who are more isolated or uneducated. They spread tales of monsters lurking in the city’s shadows, exaggerating minor incidents into threats of dark forces at work. This tactic causes people to isolate themselves, drawing them away from communal support.

## Drakren’s Temptations and Gifts

Drakren’s gifts are centered around amplifying fear, creating illusions, and manipulating people’s perceptions. He rewards his followers with abilities that allow them to intimidate others, obscure the truth, and control people through their deepest fears. These gifts are particularly appealing to those who seek power through control rather than force.

- Veil of Dread: Followers of Drakren can summon an aura of fear, making those around them feel anxious and uneasy. This aura subtly influences people’s thoughts, making them more likely to mistrust others and suspect hidden dangers.
- Eyes of the Hidden: Drakren’s chosen gain the ability to see into others’ fears, sensing what makes people uneasy or fearful. This allows them to manipulate situations to exploit those specific fears, causing their targets to question their own actions or motivations.
- Shadow of Doubt: Certain high-ranking followers are granted the Shadow of Doubt, a power that allows them to obscure the truth. With this ability, they can create illusions or distort memories, making it difficult for others to discern what is real. This gift is particularly effective in creating paranoia, as people struggle to trust even their own perceptions.

## Church Countermeasures Against Drakren

The Order of Valor and [[Order of Wisdom]] work tirelessly to counter Drakren’s influence, recognizing the dangers of fear and mistrust to the stability of Aru’Mas. The [[Order of Harmony]] also contributes by offering support to those who have fallen prey to paranoia or are struggling with anxiety, providing a sense of safety and community.

- Public Displays of Courage and Unity: The Order of Valor holds regular demonstrations and parades that showcase the city’s strength and unity. By displaying acts of courage and solidarity, they remind citizens that they are protected and encourage them to resist the isolation and fear that Drakren promotes.
- The Vigil of Light: The Order of Wisdom hosts a monthly ceremony called the Vigil of Light, where citizens gather to confront their fears in a supportive environment. During the ceremony, people are encouraged to share their anxieties openly, and the Order of Wisdom provides guidance on overcoming irrational fears, fostering resilience and community trust.
- Ritual of Clarity: The Order of Harmony performs a Ritual of Clarity in public spaces, focusing on dispelling illusions, uncovering hidden truths, and fostering transparency. This ritual serves to counter Drakren’s influence, clearing away the mental fog that fear creates and restoring a sense of trust and connection among citizens.
- Education on Critical Thinking: The Order of Wisdom works to educate the public on critical thinking, encouraging people to question rumors and evaluate evidence before succumbing to fear. By promoting reason and understanding, they aim to reduce the hold that paranoia and superstition have over the people of Aru’Mas.

## Cultural Myths and Fear of Drakren

Drakren is often portrayed in Aru’Mas folklore as a figure who lurks in the shadows, watching and waiting for moments of weakness to strike. Tales of him are told to warn against giving in to fear and suspicion, encouraging people to seek support from one another rather than isolate themselves.

- The Tale of the Silent Whisperer: One well-known story tells of a villager who prayed to Drakren to reveal the secrets of his neighbors. Though granted his wish, he soon found himself consumed by the knowledge he had gained, suspecting everyone around him of betrayal. Unable to trust anyone, he withdrew from society, eventually becoming a prisoner of his own fear. This story is often told to emphasize the dangers of allowing fear and suspicion to control one’s actions.
- The “Shadowed Eyes” in the Fend: In the Fend, Drakren’s influence is known as the “Shadowed Eyes,” a term used to describe the phenomenon where people become paranoid and suspicious, seeing threats where none exist. Fendfolk ward against the Shadowed Eyes by keeping symbols of unity, such as woven circles or communal charms, in their homes. These symbols are believed to protect against Drakren’s influence and remind families of their bond.

## Summary

Drakren, the Architect of Fear, is a deity who thrives on paranoia, isolation, and the erosion of trust. Through his followers, the Silent Eyes, he seeks to foster a culture of fear and suspicion, where people are encouraged to question and distrust those around them. The Silent Eyes operate in secrecy, spreading rumors, instigating paranoia, and manipulating the truth to achieve Drakren’s goals. Despite Drakren’s influence, the Orders of Valor, Wisdom, and Harmony work tirelessly to counter his effects by promoting courage, unity, and mental resilience. Through ceremonies like the Vigil of Light and the Ritual of Clarity, as well as public displays of strength and trust-building initiatives, the Church of the Threefold Path strives to protect the people of Aru’Mas from the shadow of fear that Drakren casts over the city.
