# Acheron

Acheron is a plane of war without purpose — enormous iron cubes adrift in a lightless void, each one a battlefield covered in the wreckage and armies of conflicts that have long since outlasted any objective they may once have served. Combatants fight here because the plane produces fighting, and because those who arrive find their capacity to stop doing so gradually eroded. The machinery of war here has functioned continuously for longer than anyone has been tracking it.

**Ahvantir connection:** No documented relationship. Acheron is occasionally referenced in [[Sunspear Legion]] historical scholarship as a cautionary cosmological concept — the risk of a military apparatus that persists beyond the cause that created it — but this is rhetorical rather than theological.
